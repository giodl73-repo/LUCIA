The concept is correct and must stay; the form can be interior to the caravan master's experience rather than the author's analysis. "The Sanhaja who had been among the taxable parties now found they had a different identity, and the crossing's tax was a payment to an authority they no longer recognized" — this is the same analysis in the chapter's own voice.
