# Sources — Hausa City-States

## Primary

- *Kano Chronicle* — the most complete Hausa king-list and historical narrative; written by court scholars
- *Katsina Chronicle* — parallel chronicle for Katsina
- Usman dan Fodio's writings — his critique of Hausa rulers and the theological basis of the jihad
- Leo Africanus, *Description of Africa*, c. 1526 — account of the Hausa states from a contemporary traveler

## Academic

- Smith, M.G. *Government in Kano 1350–1950*. 1997. Westview Press. — definitive political history
- Last, Murray. *The Sokoto Caliphate*. 1967. Longman. — the jihad and its aftermath
- Hogben, S.J. & Kirk-Greene, A.H.M. *The Emirates of Northern Nigeria*. 1966. Oxford University Press.
- Paden, John N. *Religion and Political Culture in Kano*. 1973. University of California Press.
- Lovejoy, Paul E. *Caravans of Kola: The Hausa Kola Trade 1700–1900*. 1980. Ahmadu Bello University Press.
- Christelow, Allan. *Thus Ruled Emir Abbas: Selected Cases from the Records of the Emir of Kano's Judicial Council*. 1994. Michigan State University Press.
