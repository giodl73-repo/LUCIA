**Notes:** Passes Gate-2. The chapter's central achievement is telling the story of an empire's peak and first crack simultaneously, using the quipucamayoc's perspective to render both without verdict. The epidemic section avoids the two failure modes: sentimentalizing the loss or treating it as inevitable retribution. The disease arrives as a fact, moves through the system, and is recorded by the quipucamayoc with the same care they applied to the peak. This is inside-voice decline.

**Innovation candidate:** The quipu's categorical failure as structural engine — the information system encountering an event outside its categories — may constitute a new technique for chapters about systems facing unprecedented shocks. The technique makes the information system's design (what it can record) the lens through which the reader experiences what it cannot record. Logging for observation.
