## Innovations Noted

**Innovation candidate — Prophetic-Migratory Voice:** This voice register appears here for the first time in LUCIA. It is distinct from all 21 previously confirmed voice types (griot, steppe, maritime, oral-tradition, vertical, hydraulic, sedimentary, monument, urban/scholarly, argumentative, legal-public, inscriptional, river, saga-spare, maritime accumulative, philosophical-devotional, edict-inscriptional, trade/diplomatic, chronicler, scholarly-revolutionary, urban-manufacturing). The prophetic-migratory register is defined by: (1) gathering sentences that accumulate momentum like a migration accumulates miles; (2) short declaratives that land like prophetic pronouncements; (3) a rhythm that moves but does not rush — always in the direction of something, never arrived; (4) zero em-dashes as an emergent property of a voice that finds its pauses in periods. Flag for logging in INNOVATIONS.md if a second instance confirms the pattern.

**Innovation candidate — Cosmological Direction as Structural Engine:** The chapter's structural engine is not an object, a cycle, or a person but a direction — east, toward Yvy Maraey. The direction operates as both geographic fact and cosmological reality; it is unfalsifiable (always east, even when the ocean is reached, because the Land is east of the ocean); and it functions as the tradition's persistence mechanism. This is distinct from the v1.5 Material Object engine, from the v1.1 Structural Engine, and from the v1.5 Non-Chronological Organizing Principle. A cosmological direction that cannot be falsified and therefore cannot exhaust the engine is a terminal-condition variant worth noting.
