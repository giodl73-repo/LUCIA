# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Pallava copper plate grants and stone inscriptions — Epigraphia Indica volumes
- Mahendravarman I's *Mattavilasa Prahasana* (Sanskrit play) — trans. Michael Lockwood
- Palimpsest Prakrit and Sanskrit inscriptions at Mamallapuram

## Key Academic Works

- K.R. Srinivasan, *Cave Temples of the Pallavas* (1964)
- Padma Kaimal — Pallava portraiture and royal imagery
- Padma Kaimal, *Scattered Goddesses* (2011)
- Michael Lockwood, *Mahabalipuram and the Pallavas* (1982)
- Hermann Kulke and Dietmar Rothermund, *A History of India* (2004)

- Architectural analysis connecting Pallava forms to Southeast Asian adaptations
- Recent epigraphic work on the Mamallapuram inscription corpus
