# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Prithvi Narayan Shah's *Divya Upadesh* (Divine Counsel) — the founding king's political testament
- Anglo-Nepalese War records — British India Office records
- Treaty of Sugauli (1816)
- Brian Hodgson's papers — British Resident in Nepal, 19th century

## Key Academic Works

- John Whelpton, *A History of Nepal* (2005)
- Ludwig Stiller, *The Rise of the House of Gorkha* (1973)
- Leo Rose, *Nepal: Strategy for Survival* (1971)
- Pratyoush Onta — Nepali historiography and nationalism
- Joanna Pfaff-Czarnecka — ethnicity and nationhood in Nepal
