# B-armenian-3 -- Robert W. Thomson

**Expertise:** Classical Armenian literature; translations of the fifth-century historians (Agathangelos, Movses Khorenatsi, Yeghishe, Koriwn, Ghazar Parpetsi); the Golden Age of Armenian letters.

**Key Works:** Translations and critical editions of Agathangelos, Movses Khorenatsi, Yeghishe, and others; *Studies in Armenian Literature and Christianity* (Variorum).

**Lens:** The accuracy of the literary claims. The characterization of the fifth-century historians. The description of the translation project. The relationship between Koriwn's account and historical Mashtots.
