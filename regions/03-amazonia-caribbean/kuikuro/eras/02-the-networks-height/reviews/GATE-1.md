## Assessment

A perfect gate score. The opening does exactly what the hook promised: the road as diplomatic infrastructure, the maintenance act as network-maintenance technology. The voice is discovered, not imposed — it grows from what road-clearing actually is: patient, repetitive, purposeful work that exists because someone decided to keep something alive.

**Innovations to note:**
- **Garden-Town voice**: A new register for settled forest-agriculture cultures — not maritime, not steppe, not river. Patient, labor-attentive, thinking-through-maintenance. The prose rhythm matches the physical rhythm of sustained, purposeful agricultural management. May be worth logging.
- **Infrastructure-as-commitment**: The road as diplomatic announcement in physical form. Not symbolic — causal. A maintained road means arrival. This is the structural engine showing its face in the opening.
