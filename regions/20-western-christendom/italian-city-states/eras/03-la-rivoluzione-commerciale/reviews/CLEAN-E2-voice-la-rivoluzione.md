## Baseline Register (from opening)

Urban/commercial. Precise, unsentimental, transactional. Medium-length sentences that accumulate like ledger entries, punctuated by short declarative stops when a principle is established. No lyricism, no academic hedging. The voice of a man who knows what things cost and what they are worth.
