# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Milarepa's Hundred Thousand Songs* — trans. Garma C.C. Chang
- *The Life of Milarepa* — trans. Lobsang P. Lhalungpa
- Sakya Pandita's *Clear Differentiation of the Three Codes*
- Tsongkhapa, *Lamrim Chenmo* (Great Treatise on the Stages of the Path) — trans. Lamrim Chenmo Translation Committee (Snow Lion, 3 vols.)

## Key Academic Works

- Matthew Kapstein, *The Tibetan Assimilation of Buddhism* (2000)
- Ronald Davidson, *Tibetan Renaissance* (2005) — second diffusion
- Leonard van der Kuijp — Sakya school studies
- Elliot Sperling — Tibetan-Mongol relations
- Sam Van Schaik, *Tibet: A History* (2011)
