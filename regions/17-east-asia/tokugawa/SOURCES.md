# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Matsuo Bashō, *Oku no Hosomichi* (Narrow Road to the Deep North) — trans. Nobuyuki Yuasa (Penguin)
- Ihara Saikaku — prose fiction; *Five Women Who Loved Love*, trans. William Theodore de Bary
- Sugita Genpaku, *Rangaku Kotohajime* (Beginning of Dutch Studies) — trans. various
- *Kōjiki-den* (Norinaga's commentary on Kojiki) — partially trans.

## Key Academic Works

- Conrad Totman, *Tokugawa Ieyasu: Shogun* (1983)
- Mary Elizabeth Berry, *Japan in Print* (2006) — Tokugawa print culture
- Timon Screech — rangaku and Dutch learning
- Luke Roberts — domain studies
- Mark Ravina — daimyo and Tokugawa governance
