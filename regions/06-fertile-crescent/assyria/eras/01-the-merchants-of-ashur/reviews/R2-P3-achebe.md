**The section plan resolves my primary concern.** There is no section devoted to "contextualizing Ashur within the larger Assyrian story." Each section inhabits the merchant world on its own terms. The landing (Section 7) acknowledges what comes next without borrowing from it. This is the correct approach.

**What I will watch for in the chapter:**

The chapter must show what survived through what the later Assyrians DID, not through a metaphor about cultural genetics.
