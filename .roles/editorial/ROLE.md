# Editorial Roles

Three lenses for the CLEAN stage (Stage 9). Unlike the panel, these are
not intellectual perspectives — they are surgical tools. Each one catches
a specific category of defect that the panel might miss because the panel
reads for quality, not for compliance.

## When They Fire

After GATE-2 scoring, before BOARD review. The chapter has already been
written, scored, and deemed good enough to proceed. The editorial pass
catches what slipped through.

## Review Output Format

Editorial reviews are terse. No discussion, no intellectual context.
Just findings:

```markdown
## E-{N} — {Name} — CLEAN pass on {chapter}

**Violations found:** {count}

1. Line {N}: "{quoted text}" — {violation type}: {explanation}
2. Line {N}: "{quoted text}" — {violation type}: {explanation}
...

**Verdict:** CLEAN / {count} fixes needed
```

## Members

- [E-1: The Judgment Auditor](E-1-judgment-auditor.md) — Outside lens, forbidden words, anachronistic morality
- [E-2: The Voice Keeper](E-2-voice-keeper.md) — Register shifts, textbook drift, modern commentary
- [E-3: The Compression Editor](E-3-compression-editor.md) — Re-explanation, filler, earned-emotion violations
