## Meta-findings

1. **Every v1.7 candidate has ≥ 3 instances.** Clears the v1.5 cluster-promotion bar. Recommendation: the 8 candidates are ready for v1.8 adoption as formalized types.

2. **The endogenous-institutional family is not confined to colonial empires.** Candidates 2, 3, 4, 6 have non-colonial instances (Samanids, Mongol, Plantagenet, Italian City-States), addressing the CLUSTER-ANALYSIS-COLONIAL-EMPIRES.md "watch for non-colonial instance" flag.

3. **Ongoing is a genuine closure mode, not a failure.** For cultures whose chapter-arc deliberately opens to the next era (Roman book, Inca book, Mali book), the close is transition, not terminal. Should be formalized in v1.8 as a valid close-type alongside the 18 terminal types.

4. **Scale-dependent pattern consistent with PR-A3 and PR-B2.** At arc-closure scale, validated-core / candidate-tail structure holds (10 formalized + 8 candidates covering 84%).

5. **4 truly novel closures** are detection-engine signal beyond 115 chapters — candidates for future v1.9+ amendments.
