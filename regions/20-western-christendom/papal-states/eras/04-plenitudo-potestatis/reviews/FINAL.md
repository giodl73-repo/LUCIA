## Structural Engine

**Engine:** Canon law as universal jurisdiction.

## Innovations Flagged for INNOVATIONS.md

**1. Canonical-juridical evidence as structural evidence (extends v1.3 "Primary Art as Structural Evidence")**
The Unam Sanctam is quoted verbatim in the chapter not as decoration but as the engine's self-performance: the culture's own legal text IS the primary evidence for what the culture claimed and how it claimed it. The quote does the analytical work the chapter would otherwise describe. This extends the v1.3 technique to juridical cultures where the legal pronouncement is simultaneously the culture's primary art form and its governing instrument.

**2. Self-undermining legitimacy loop as terminal condition (new type, extends v1.5/v1.6 taxonomy)**
The papal terminal condition is distinct from the v1.5 legitimacy gap (Songhai). In Songhai: military authority cannot generate commercial-legal legitimacy (gap between two authority types). In the papacy: the fiscal machine that enforces a single authority type undermines the spiritual credibility that authority type requires (self-undermining loop within one authority). The engine consumes its own fuel. This may warrant addition to the v1.6 terminal condition taxonomy as type 11: "self-undermining loop."
