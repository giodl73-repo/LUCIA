# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Gwanggaeto Stele (414 CE) — trans. and analysis by various Korean and Japanese scholars
- *Samguk Sagi* — Goguryeo annals; trans. Jonathan Best (partial)
- Chinese sources: *Sui Shu*, *Tang Shu* — Goguryeo campaign records
- Goguryeo tomb murals — iconographic corpus (multiple archaeological publications)

## Key Academic Works

- Ki-baik Lee, *A New History of Korea* (1984)
- Jonathan Best — early Korean history and the Three Kingdoms period
- Gina Barnes — Korean archaeology
- Mark Byington — Goguryeo studies
- Kenneth Wells — Korea's connections to continental culture
