# B-Taíno-1 — Irving Rouse

## Background

Irving Rouse (1913–2006) was the archaeologist who spent his career reconstructing the prehistory of the Caribbean from the ceramic record. His *The Tainos: Rise and Decline of the People Who Greeted Columbus* (1992) remains the essential synthesis of what the archaeological record shows about Taíno origins, development, and political organization. He worked against the narrative of Taíno extinction throughout his career.
