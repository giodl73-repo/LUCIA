# B-armenian-1 -- Nina Garsoian

**Expertise:** Ancient and early medieval Armenian history; Armenia between Byzantium and the Sasanians; the Arsacid period; the conversion; the nakharar system.

**Key Works:** *Armenia Between Byzantium and the Sasanians* (1997); contributions to *The Armenian People from Ancient to Modern Times* (ed. Hovannisian); extensive work on the Arsacid period and Armenian-Iranian relations.

**Lens:** Historical accuracy of the pre-alphabet centuries. The political structures of the Arsacid kingdom. The relationship between the Armenian aristocracy and the imperial powers. The conversion as both spiritual and political event.
