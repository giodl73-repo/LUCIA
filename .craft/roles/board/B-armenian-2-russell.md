# B-armenian-2 -- James R. Russell

**Expertise:** Armenian language, literature, and religion; Zoroastrianism in Armenia; the pre-Christian Armenian religious world; the Armenian alphabet and early literature.

**Key Works:** *Zoroastrianism in Armenia* (Harvard Iranian Series); extensive publications on Armenian linguistics, literature, and the transition from paganism to Christianity.

**Lens:** The linguistic accuracy of the alphabet discussion. The pre-Christian religious context. The relationship between the old gods (Aramazd, Anahit, Vahagn) and the new faith. The accuracy of the phonological description.
