# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Rigveda* — trans. Wendy Doniger (Penguin Classics) or Stephanie Jamison/Joel Brereton (Oxford)
- *Atharvaveda* — trans. Maurice Bloomfield
- *Upanishads* — trans. Patrick Olivelle (Oxford World's Classics, 1996)
- *Mahabharata* — trans. J.A.B. van Buitenen (partial) and Bibek Debroy (complete)

## Key Academic Works

- Witzel, Michael — early Vedic culture and chronology (Harvard Indo-Iranian journal articles)
- Wendy Doniger, *The Rig Veda* (Penguin, 1981)
- Patrick Olivelle, *The Early Upanisads* (1998)
- A.L. Basham, *The Wonder That Was India* (1954) — dated but foundational
- Romila Thapar, *Early India* (2002) — critical reappraisal
