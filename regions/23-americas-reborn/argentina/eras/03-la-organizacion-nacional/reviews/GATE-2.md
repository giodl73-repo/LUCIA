## Innovations to Flag

**Potential innovation — Syncretic Knowledge System as Structural Engine:** The tango-as-knowledge-system (Section III, "The Milonga") shows a syncretic cultural form not as the product of ethnic mixing but as a social technology for navigating proximity. The chapter renders the dance as a method — the close embrace as a grammar of weight and balance developed by necessity, not by aesthetic intention. This may warrant logging in INNOVATIONS.md as a variation on the v1.3 Body-as-Instrument technique: not a hunting or navigational body-knowledge but an interpersonal-navigation body-knowledge specific to mass immigration's social conditions.

**Potential innovation — European-Validation Loop as Terminal Condition:** The engine does not break; it is outgrown. The loop persists but loses its governing function when the electoral arithmetic changes. This may be a new terminal-condition type: **democratic supersession** — the engine's controlling mechanism (validation from outside) is not destroyed but rendered politically irrelevant by the mass it produced. Distinct from legitimacy gap (which fails to maintain authority) — democratic supersession maintains authority and then democratically transfers it. Should be logged and watched for a second instance.
