## Register Breaks
- Section II: "the irony that the Romani later expelled their kings while retaining everything the kings had built was not lost on them." The word "irony" introduces a modern analytical frame. The Romani would not have called it irony. Suggest: "That the Romani later expelled their kings while retaining everything the kings had built was a fact the tradition absorbed without comment."
- Section VI: "the garum fermenting in the pottery factories south of the Aventine, the fish sauce whose stench was matched only by its ubiquity on Roman tables, a condiment so essential to Roman cooking that its production would eventually become an industry spanning the Mediterranean." This sentence is charming but breaks the legal register with a more essayistic, almost humorous tone. The sentence can stay but should lose the "whose stench was matched only by its ubiquity" construction.

## Voice Consistency Assessment
The legal-public register is sustained through 95%+ of the chapter. The institutional sections (IV, V) are the strongest voice moments. The narrative sections (I, III) maintain register while adding sensory detail. The landing is the voice at its purest: short sentences, each closing like a verdict.
