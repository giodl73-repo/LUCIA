## Chronicle Verdict

**56/60 — PASSES GATE-2**

Target: 48+/60. Combined: 56 + 29 = 85/90.

**Exceptional dimensions:** Voice (10/10) — the Capitulary register is a new
confirmed voice type not present in the v1.4 spectrum. Landing (10/10) —
the landing is structurally inevitable. Evidence (9/10) — knowledge system
at method level achieved for the scriptorium section.

**Innovations to flag:**

1. **Capitulary Voice Register** (Voice): A new register between Court and
   Edict-Inscriptional: declarative main clause, brief qualification, return
   to the directive rhythm. The voice of a reform program that believes in
   what it commands. Distinct from Court (too layered), Edict-Inscriptional
   (too outward-facing), and Legal-Public (too forensic). The zero-em-dash
   score is an emergent property of the register, not a separate discipline.

2. **Reform Program as Structural Engine** (Progression): The *correctio*
   mechanism (literacy as medium of imperial administration, running on
   personal authority, terminating when that authority cannot be transmitted)
   is a new terminal-condition type candidate: **personal-authority collapse**.
   Distinct from legitimacy gap (Songhai: military authority can't generate
   commercial-legal legitimacy) because here the authority is fully legitimate
   but non-transferable. The engine performs correctly until the person who
   animated it is gone; it then stops, while its products (the manuscripts,
   the script, the idea) continue independently. This is a new terminal
   condition: **vindicating-but-non-transferable**: the reform succeeds in its
   products while failing in its system. Log for innovation consideration.
