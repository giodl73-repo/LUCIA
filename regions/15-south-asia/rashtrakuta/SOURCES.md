# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Sanjan copper plates (Amoghavarsha I's genealogy)
- Arab merchant Sulaiman's account (~851 CE) — in Ibn Khurradadhbih, trans. various
- *Kavirajamarga* (attributed to Amoghavarsha I) — earliest Kannada poetics text
- Ellora cave inscriptions — Kailasa temple dedication records

## Key Academic Works

- A.S. Altekar, *The Rashtrakutas and their Times* (1934)
- Hermann Kulke and Dietmar Rothermund, *A History of India* (2004)
- Ronald Inden — Kanauj triangle studies
- Sheldon Pollock, *The Language of the Gods in the World of Men* (2006) — Sanskrit/Kannada
- George Michell, *The Penguin Guide to the Monuments of India* (1989) — Ellora
