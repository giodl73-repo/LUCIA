# Sources — Great Zimbabwe

## Primary / Archaeological

- Randall-MacIver, David. *Mediaeval Rhodesia*. 1906. — the first archaeological proof of African construction
- Caton-Thompson, Gertrude. *The Zimbabwe Culture: Ruins and Reactions*. 1931. — the definitive pre-WWII analysis
- Garlake, Peter. *Great Zimbabwe*. 1973. Thames & Hudson. — the modern archaeological synthesis
- Zimbabwe ruins excavation records — National Museums and Monuments of Zimbabwe

## Academic

- Garlake, Peter S. *Great Zimbabwe: Described and Explained*. 1973. Thames & Hudson.
- Huffman, Tom N. *Snakes and Crocodiles: Power and Symbolism in Ancient Zimbabwe*. 1996. Witwatersrand University Press.
- Beach, D.N. *The Shona and Zimbabwe 900–1850*. 1980. Heinemann. — the most thorough historical synthesis
- Pikirayi, Innocent. *The Zimbabwe Culture: Origins and Decline of Southern Zambezian States*. 2001. AltaMira Press.
- Ranger, Terence. "The Invention of Tradition in Colonial Africa." In *The Invention of Tradition*, ed. Hobsbawm & Ranger. 1983. Cambridge University Press. — the colonial denial analyzed
- Chirikure, Shadreck. *Great Zimbabwe: Unfolding the Mystery*. 2020. Springer. — the most recent comprehensive archaeological study
