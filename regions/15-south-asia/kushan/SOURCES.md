# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Kushan coin corpus — comprehensive numismatic collections (British Museum, Kabul Museum)
- Rabatak inscription (Bactrian) — Kanishka's foundation record
- Kharoshthi and Brahmi inscriptions from Taxila and Mathura

## Key Academic Works

- B.N. Mukherjee, *The Rise and Fall of the Kushana Empire* (1988)
- Nicholas Sims-Williams — Bactrian language and Kushan epigraphy
- Étienne de la Vaissière, *Sogdian Traders: A History* (2005)
- John Rosenfield, *The Dynastic Arts of the Kushans* (1967)
- Kurt Behrendt, *The Art of Gandhara* (Metropolitan Museum, 2007)
