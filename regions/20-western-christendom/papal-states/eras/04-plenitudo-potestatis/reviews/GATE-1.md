## Notes

Strong opening. The legal-public voice is fully discovered and sustained without a single break. The dual-dating of the year (Apostolic chapter / years from Christ) does the World work without explanation — the reader feels the Church's own reckoning of time without being taught it.

The one Honesty caution (Norse conversion framing) is P3 borderline. The sentence lists delegates from multiple regions; the Norse phrasing is describing geographic reach, not making a civilizational judgment. Does not require revision before proceeding, but the Panel may flag it.

The Texture score could push to 5/5 with a single sensory anchor from the physical hall — the Lateran in 1215 was a specific, documented space, and one detail (the press of bodies, the heat of a thousand candles, the Latin cadences echoing off stone) would ground the abstract weight of the canons in a felt moment. Flagged for author awareness at FIXES stage.

**GATE-1 RESULT: 28/30. PASS. Proceed to PANEL-1.**
