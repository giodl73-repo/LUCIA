# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Chinese records on P'o-ni (Ming Veritable Records, tribute documentation)
- Brunei royal genealogies (*silsilah*)
- Spanish accounts of the 1578 Brunei campaign
- *Salasilah Raja-raja Brunei* — the Brunei royal chronicle

## Key Academic Works

- D.E. Brown, *Brunei: The Structure and History of a Bornean Malay Sultanate* (1970) — foundational
- Graham Saunders, *A History of Brunei* (2nd ed., 2002)
- Robert Nicholl — Brunei history and European sources
- Victor King — Borneo peoples and Brunei's regional context
- Rozan Yunos — Brunei genealogy and court history
