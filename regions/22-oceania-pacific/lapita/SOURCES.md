# Sources — Lapita

## Key Academic Works
- Peter Bellwood, *The Austronesians: Historical and Comparative Perspectives* (1995, ANU Press) — the foundational account of Austronesian expansion
- Matthew Spriggs, *The Island Melanesians* (1997, Blackwell) — Lapita in its Melanesian context
- Patrick Kirch, *On the Road of the Winds: An Archaeological History of the Pacific Islands before European Contact* (2000, University of California Press) — the standard comprehensive treatment
- Patrick Kirch & Roger Green, *Hawaiki, Ancestral Polynesia: An Essay in Historical Anthropology* (2001, Cambridge University Press) — Lapita-to-Polynesia transition
- David Burley et al. — Tonga Lapita site excavations; dating and ceramic analysis
- Stuart Bedford — Vanuatu Lapita sites; eastern expansion chronology

## Archaeological Sources
- Lapita sites at Mussau Islands (Bismarcks) — Kirch excavations; foundational
- Site SE-RF-2, Reef/Santa Cruz Islands — the open-ocean crossing evidence
- Nukuleka site, Tonga — Ancestral Polynesian Society development zone
- Bismarck Archipelago obsidian sourcing studies — trade network reconstruction

## Needed (ACQUISITION)
- Updated genetic studies on Lapita population origins (ancient DNA from Vanuatu skeletons — Skoglund et al. 2016, *Nature*)
- Canoe construction and sailing performance studies for the Lapita voyaging period
- More complete ceramic sequence analysis for the Tonga-Samoa developmental horizon
