# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Tiruvalangadu copper plates (Rajendra I's genealogy and conquests)
- Leyden grant (Rajendra I's Southeast Asian naval expedition)
- Tamil Tevaram hymns — Nayanmars' devotional poetry; trans. Indira Peterson
- Kalinkattupparani — war poem on Kulottunga's Kalinga expedition

## Key Academic Works

- K.A. Nilakanta Sastri, *The Colas* (2nd ed., 1955) — foundational
- Noboru Karashima, *A Concise History of South India* (2014)
- Cynthia Talbot, *Precolonial India in Practice* (2001)
- George Spencer — Chola naval expedition studies
- Leslie Orr, *Donors, Devotees, and Daughters of God* (2000) — temple women

## Needed
