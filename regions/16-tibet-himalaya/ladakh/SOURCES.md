# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Ladakhi royal chronicles (*rgyal-rabs*) — Francke's translations
- Treaty of Tingmosgang (1684) — text and analysis in Petech
- East India Company reports on the Dogra conquest of Ladakh

## Key Academic Works

- Luciano Petech, *The Kingdom of Ladakh* (1977) — fundamental study
- August Hermann Francke, *A History of Ladakh* (1907, repr. 1977)
- John Bray — Ladakhi history and Christianity
- Janet Rizvi, *Ladakh: Crossroads of High Asia* (1983)
- Kim Gutschow — Ladakhi Buddhist monasticism
