## Notes

Gate passed at 30/30. An exceptional opening. Several features worth flagging for the innovation log:

1. **Dual-protagonist structure as legal argument**: The opening does not alternate between Ferdomnach and Tírechán for dramatic contrast — it positions them as parallel practitioners of the same function (memory-keeping) in different media. The structural argument (oral and written as co-existing technologies, not successive stages) is embedded in the scene itself without being stated. This is honesty operating at the culture-logic level (v1.2 standard).

2. **Honor-price as narrative lever**: The lóg n-enech calculus (seven cumals vs. six, the éraic calculation governing praise order) is not background — it is the plot mechanism that determines what Ferdomnach sings and why, and what Conall's response might mean. The legal system is the story's engine, operating at method level (v1.5 standard: Knowledge System at Method Level). This should carry through to the chapter.

3. **Zero em-dashes**: The filí-cadence produces its own pause mechanism through the period and the comma-chain. This is not a counted absence — it is an emergent property of the voice.

**Recommendation**: Proceed to PANEL-1 without revision.
