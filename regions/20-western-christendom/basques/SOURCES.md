# Sources — Basques

## Primary Sources

- *Fuero General de Navarra* — 13th century; the major Navarrese-Basque law code
- *Fuero de Vizcaya* — 15th century written compilation of Basque custom law
- *Aralar Codex* and Basque medieval charters — property and legal records in Basque and Latin
- Juan de la Cosa's *Mappa Mundi* (1500) — the first map showing the New World; made by a Basque pilot
- Basque whaling records from Newfoundland and Labrador — 16th century; excavated at Red Bay

## Secondary Sources

- Collins, Roger. *The Basques*. Blackwell, 1986.
- Kurlansky, Mark. *The Basque History of the World*. Walker, 1999.
- Douglass, William A. and Jon Bilbao. *Amerikanuak: Basques in the New World*. University of Nevada Press, 1975.
- Trask, R.L. *The History of Basque*. Routledge, 1997.
- Orella Unzué, José Luis. *El origen de la Hermandad de Álava*. 1976.
- Huxley, Selma. *Los Vascos en el marco Atlántico norte*. Etor, 1987.
- Barkham, Selma Huxley. *The Basque Coast of Newfoundland*. Great Northern Peninsula Development Corporation, 1989.
