# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Shaybatnama* (c. 1510) — court history of Muhammad Shaybani Khan
- Abu'l-Ghazi Bahadur Khan, *Shajara-i Türk* (Genealogy of the Turks, 1663) — Khiva ruler's history
- Bukharan chroniclers (Mir Muhammad Amin Bukhari and others) — local dynastic histories
- Russian diplomatic and intelligence records — the tsarist assessment of the Central Asian khanates

## Key Academic Works

- Robert McChesney, *Waqf in Central Asia: Four Hundred Years in the History of a Muslim Shrine* (1991)
- Audrey Burton, *The Bukharans: A Dynastic, Diplomatic and Commercial History, 1550–1702* (1997)
- Scott Levi, *The Indian Diaspora in Central Asia and Its Trade, 1550–1900* (2002)
- Adeeb Khalid, *The Politics of Muslim Cultural Reform: Jadidism in Central Asia* (1998)
- Alexander Morrison — Russian conquest of Central Asia
