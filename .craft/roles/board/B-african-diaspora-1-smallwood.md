# B-1 — Stephanie Smallwood

Associate Professor of History, University of Washington. *Saltwater Slavery* (Harvard, 2007) is the definitive study of the Middle Passage as a process of social and ontological transformation. Smallwood's work examines how the slave trade converted persons into commodities and how the captives resisted and survived that conversion. Her expertise covers the Gold Coast trade, the barracoons, the ship, and the process of arriving in the Americas.
