# Sources — Shona

## Primary / Oral

- Shona oral traditions — maintained by community historians and spirit medium specialists
- Gelfand, Michael. Early ethnographic accounts of Shona religious practice
- Spirit medium testimonies — collected by David Lan for *Guns and Rain*

## Academic

- Beach, D.N. *The Shona and Zimbabwe 900–1850*. 1980. Heinemann. — the foundational historical study
- Beach, D.N. *A Zimbabwean Past: Shona Dynastic Histories and Oral Traditions*. 1994. Mambo Press.
- Lan, David. *Guns and Rain: Guerrillas and Spirit Mediums in Zimbabwe*. 1985. University of California Press. — the spirit medium-liberation war connection
- Bourdillon, M.F.C. *The Shona Peoples: An Ethnography of the Contemporary Shona*. 3rd ed. 1987. Mambo Press.
- Ranger, Terence. *Revolt in Southern Rhodesia 1896–7: A Study in African Resistance*. 1967. Heinemann.
- Chirikure, Shadreck. *Great Zimbabwe: Unfolding the Mystery*. 2020. Springer. — archaeological context
