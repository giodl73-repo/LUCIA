# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Zhabdrung's writings* — Drukpa Kagyu texts
- Bhutan National Archives — dzong-era administrative records
- George Bogle and Samuel Turner's accounts (18th-century British missions to Bhutan)
- Annual Reports of the Political Officer in Sikkim (for British-era records)

## Key Academic Works

- Michael Aris, *Bhutan: The Early History of a Himalayan Kingdom* (1979)
- Leo Rose, *The Politics of Bhutan* (1977)
- Françoise Pommaret — Bhutanese art and festival studies
- Karma Phuntsho, *The History of Bhutan* (2013)
- John Ardussi — Bhutanese historical archives

## Needed

- Studies on dzong architecture and administrative function
- Recent scholarship on the Zhabdrung's military and religious unification
