## Voice Register Classification

**Provisional:** "Structural-Violence" (discover variant within Rupture & Diaspora family)

Characteristics:
- Political violence as ecological condition (named as such by the culture — "La Violencia")
- Matter-of-fact narration of the extraordinary and the murderous
- Accumulative sentence structure; pause mechanism: the period
- The ordinary and the extraordinary at identical narrative height
- No explanatory pause; no gasping at the violence
- Objects as memory carriers (the cast-iron pot, the grandmother's saint, the little gold fish)
- The political rendered through the personal without the personal disappearing into the political
