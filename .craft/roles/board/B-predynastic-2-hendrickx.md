# B-predynastic-2 — Stan Hendrickx

## Background

Stan Hendrickx is a Belgian Egyptologist specializing in Predynastic
archaeology, particularly the ceramic traditions and cultural chronology
of the Naqada period. His work on the Oxford History of Ancient Egypt
chapter on Prehistory is a foundational reference for the period.

## Key Question

**"Are the archaeological facts right?"**

## What He Will Praise

- Accurate ceramic descriptions
- Correct use of the Naqada sequence dating
- Proper geographic framing (Upper Egypt focus for Naqada I)
- Honest treatment of uncertainty in obsidian sourcing and chronology

## What He Will Challenge

- Any misdescription of White Cross-Lined Ware technique or iconography
- Incorrect site attributions or geographic claims
- Overstatement of certainty about trade mechanisms
- Conflation of Naqada I and Naqada II material culture
