# B-Kalinago-1 — Neil Whitehead

Neil Whitehead (1956–2012) was the leading scholar of Carib/Kalinago history
and ethnohistory. His *Lords of the Tiger Spirit* (1988) remains the definitive
scholarly reconstruction of the colonial-period Kalinago political world, built
from careful reading of European sources against their grain. He understood
the Kalinago raiding economy as a political system, not a cultural pathology,
and he was responsible for much of the scholarly dismantling of the "cannibal"
construct.
