**Notes:** GATE-1 passed. The opening invents a voice register not yet in the Voice Spectrum: call it "Linguistic-Nomadic" or "Discover." The prose moves like the journey it describes, accumulating borrowed vocabulary the way the Romani language accumulated loanwords. Scene scores 4 rather than 5 because the anchoring is conceptual (a word) rather than physical (a body). This may be the right choice for a culture whose relationship to its origin is through language, not through place-memory. Texture similarly trades physical sensation for linguistic materiality. Both scores are defensible as voice-appropriate rather than as deficits.

Potential innovation: **Language as material evidence** — the Romani words themselves function as primary art (v1.3/v1.5 amendment), doing the analytical work that citations would do in a documented culture. The words ARE the sources.
