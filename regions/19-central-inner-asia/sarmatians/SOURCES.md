# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Strabo, *Geography* — systematic description of Sarmatian peoples
- Tacitus, *Germania* and *Annals* — references to Sarmatian-Roman frontier interactions
- Ammianus Marcellinus — accounts of the Alans and Hunnic disruption
- Roman imperial inscriptions — documentation of Sarmatian federates in the Roman army

## Key Academic Works

- Tadeusz Sulimirski, *The Sarmatians* (1970) — foundational survey
- Askold Ivantchik — Sarmatian archaeology
- Richard Brzezinski and Mariusz Mielczarek, *The Sarmatians 600 BC–AD 450* (2002, Osprey)
- Nicola Di Cosmo — Sarmatians and the steppe world
- C. Scott Littleton and Linda Malcor, *From Scythia to Camelot* (1994) — the Sarmatian-Arthurian connection thesis (speculative but influential)

## Needed
