# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Allahabad Pillar inscription (Samudragupta's prashasti) — trans. Fleet, Epigraphia Indica
- Faxian (Fa-hien), *Record of Buddhist Kingdoms* — trans. James Legge (1886)
- Kalidasa — *Meghaduta*, *Abhijnanashakuntalam*, *Raghuvamsham* — multiple translations
- Aryabhata, *Aryabhatiya* — trans. Walter Clark (1930)

## Key Academic Works

- Romila Thapar, *The Penguin History of Early India* (2002)
- R.C. Majumdar (ed.), *The Classical Age* (History and Culture of the Indian People, vol. III)
- Upinder Singh, *A History of Ancient and Early Medieval India* (2008)
- Devangana Desai — Gupta period sculpture studies
- Kim Plofker, *Mathematics in India* (Princeton, 2009)
