# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Ashoka's Rock Edicts and Pillar Edicts — trans. Romila Thapar or Patrick Olivelle
- Kautilya, *Arthashastra* — trans. L.N. Rangarajan (Penguin) or Patrick Olivelle (2013)
- Megasthenes, *Indica* (fragmentary) — in McCrindle's ancient India sources
- *Mudrarakshasa* — Sanskrit play on Chandragupta's rise

## Key Academic Works

- Romila Thapar, *Ashoka and the Decline of the Mauryas* (1961, revised 1997)
- Patrick Olivelle, *Ashoka: Portrait of a Philosopher King* (2021)
- Upinder Singh, *A History of Ancient and Early Medieval India* (2008)
- R. Thapar, *The Penguin History of Early India* (2002)

## Needed

- Recent archaeological surveys of the Pataliputra capital site
- Comparative studies of Ashokan edicts in their regional language contexts
