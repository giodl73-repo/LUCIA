# R2-P2 — Ibn Khaldun
## Panel Review (NOTES): The Classic Taíno

The production bible is strong. The structural engine is correctly identified: the areíto as legitimacy generator, the material base as the engine's fuel, the reciprocity loop as the chapter's organizing mechanism. The terminal condition follows logically from the engine: remove the material base (epidemic, enslavement), scatter the community (the only transmission medium), seize the zemís (the activation objects that make performance meaningful), and the archive becomes inaccessible rather than destroyed. This is the correct analysis.

**Where the chapter must be careful:**

The section plan puts the degradation (Section 5) in the Classic era chapter, but the 1492 arrival is technically Era 4 material. The notes have correctly kept this to the first stress test — the seizure of the first zemís, the La Navidad garrison and its burning — as a way of showing how the Classic system responds to unprecedented pressure before the full collapse. This is justified as long as the chapter does not cross into the Era 4 narrative. The line: show the system's first encounter with what it was not designed for, then the landing returns to the Classic era's own terms.

**The Kalinago question:**

Section 4 mentions Kalinago pressure as "managing stress." I want to understand this more precisely. Was the batey used directly as a mechanism for Taíno communities to manage Kalinago relations, or is it strictly internal to Taíno inter-cacicazgo politics? If the former, say so specifically. If the latter, explain what the actual mechanism was for managing the eastern frontier. Vagueness here will produce a weak section.

**The 1496 Bartholomew Columbus reception:**

Excellent choice for Section 2. Peter Martyr's account should be used as outsider-as-structural-evidence: what Bartholomew saw and described tells us the structure of the ceremonial system, while his interpretation of it tells us about Spanish categories that the Taíno were not using. Make the bias visible and productive.

**Overall:** The engine is sound. Execute it.
