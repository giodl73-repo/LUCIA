# Panel-2 Review — Ibn Khaldun
## Hausa Era 3: The Manufacturing Peak (v2 notes)

**Focus:** Structural engine, terminal condition, causal mechanism

The production bible has named the engine correctly: competitive specialization as trust architecture. But I want to push on the mechanism more precisely before the chapter is written.

**The Songhai hinge:** The notes flag the 1591 collapse as the inflection point. This must be rendered in the chapter as a specific shift, not a background fact. The chapter should show one route that previously terminated at Gao or Timbuktu now terminating at Kano. This is the difference between Kano as a significant player in the Saharan trade and Kano as the dominant one. The Chronicle may have this; if not, the commercial logic can carry it without specific documentary support, provided the chapter signals it as inference from the known pattern.

**The self-consuming logic:** The notes identify this correctly — the Maliki legal tradition provides both the commercial infrastructure and the critique. But the chapter must be careful about the timeline. The Fulani critique does not reach its revolutionary expression until the eighteenth and early nineteenth centuries. The chapter covers 1500–1700. The terminal condition is assembling, not arriving. The scholar in Section 5 is planting seeds, not starting a fire. This distinction matters: the chapter must close at the system's peak, not at its decline, because the peak is the story. The decline belongs to Era 4.

**On the interdependence:** The notes' description of Kano-Katsina mutual dependence is analytically sound. Kano produces; Katsina makes the production trusteable at distance. But the chapter should show what happens when this interdependence is stressed — a moment where the system is almost not held together, and then is. This would be the "complication" Tuchman also demands. A dispute between Kano and Katsina merchants that the scholars have to adjudicate. The system holding because it has the infrastructure to hold.

**Verdict:** The notes are ready to drive a chapter. The terminal condition is correctly placed (assembling within the system's logic, not arriving from outside). Execute.
