## Assessment

**Passes GATE-1. Target: 24+/30. Score: 27/30.**

### Strengths

The voice is the most distinctive element: deliberate, genealogical, and weighted. The tradition's own logic drives the narration — Lueji is the subject, the choice is the act of sovereignty, the transmission is the founding. The Honesty score is high because the text never steps outside the worldview; even the line "She ruled. The tradition does not present this as unusual" is a structural honesty move that grounds No-Verdicts in the culture's own practice rather than in editorial restraint.

The lukanu is working as the structural engine from the first paragraph. The object is introduced through its material properties before its political meaning, which is correct: texture before label.

### Flags for PANEL-1

1. **World (4/5):** The ancestors' presence in the object — the way the lukanu accumulates the authority of all previous holders — is implied but not fully felt. The reader knows it intellectually before feeling it. Panel may push.

2. **Texture (4/5):** Chibinda Ilunga's arrival and person are described functionally (skilled with the bow, from Luba, carrying the bulopwe) but not sensorially. Kapuscinski will note this: where is his body? The direction of his arrival, the look of him, the feeling of the meeting.

3. **Pull (4/5):** The opening delivers Lueji as a presence but Chibinda Ilunga remains somewhat abstract. The encounter between them is stated more than staged. Tuchman may want more of the moment of decision — not romantic elaboration but the actual scene of their recognition.
