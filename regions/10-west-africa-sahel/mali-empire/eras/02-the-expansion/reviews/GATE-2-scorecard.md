## Innovations Flagged

**1. Institutional Resilience as Structural Engine.** The chapter uses the Kurukan Fuga's self-correcting mechanism as the narrative engine. Each succession crisis reveals a new institutional feature (assembly deposition power, elevation beyond lineage, survival without the king), and the accumulation of these features IS the chapter's progression. This extends the v1.1 "Covenant as Structural Engine" innovation from Era 3 into a different mode: where Era 3 used the covenant's self-consuming redistribution logic, Era 2 uses its self-correcting governance logic. Same institution, different narrative function. Worth watching — if this pattern recurs, it suggests the structural engine innovation is more versatile than the initial formulation implied.

**2. The Open Silence as Narrative Technique.** The chapter's treatment of Abu Bakr II's Atlantic voyage introduces a specific technique: leaving a narrative thread deliberately open because the tradition leaves it open. "The jelilu do not say he died. They say he did not come back." This is not ambiguity for literary effect. It is the oral tradition's own epistemological practice rendered as narrative technique. The chapter respects the tradition's distinction between known and unknown, and this respect becomes a source of narrative power. Related to the Honest Limits innovation but distinct: this is not about gaps in the record but about the tradition's deliberate choice to keep a story open.

## Overall Assessment

This chapter succeeds most fully in its structural engine (the covenant as self-correcting mechanism), its sustained voice, and its landing. The progression is genuine: each section builds the argument that the Manden was a covenant, not a dynasty, and each section adds institutional evidence the reader did not have before. The opening is exceptional — "The canoes came back wrong" may be the strongest first line in the project so far. The figures are constrained by the sources but handled honestly. The Dyula section grounds the commercial expansion in physical and ecological specificity. The landing connects to Era 3 without summarizing or overreaching.

The chapter's greatest risk was the "middle era" problem: an era between founding drama and golden-age peak that lacks a defining event. The chapter solves this by making the absence of a defining event the point. The Manden's expansion era is not the story of a great king. It is the story of a great institution tested by imperfect kings and proven by its survival. The structural engine transforms a potential weakness (no single dramatic focal point) into the chapter's central argument.
