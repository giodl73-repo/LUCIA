**Potential innovations:**
1. **Language as primary source material** (Evidence): In a culture without chronicles, the language itself functions as the primary source. Romani words are used not as decoration or flavor but as archaeological evidence, with loanword stratigraphy doing the analytical work that carbon dating does for material cultures. This extends the v1.3 "Knowledge-System-as-Evidence" amendment into a new domain: linguistic evidence as primary art.
2. **Discover voice register** (Voice): A new voice type not yet in the Voice Spectrum: accumulative, forward-moving, mythic-linguistic realism. The prose moves like the journey it describes, gathering layers. Distinct from oral-tradition (which performs) and sedimentary (which archives). The discover voice discovers: it reveals what the language has been carrying all along.
3. **Portable civilization as structural engine** (Progression): A structural engine built on intangibility: "You cannot burn a language, you cannot confiscate a skill, you cannot demolish a kinship system." This is a new engine type: the survival engine that works precisely because it has no material form to be destroyed.
