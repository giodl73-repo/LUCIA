# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Zeami, *Fushikaden* and other Noh theory works — trans. Rimer and Yamazaki
- Sesshū Tōyō — ink painting biography and works (Freer-Sackler collection)
- *Onin Ki* — chronicle of the Ōnin War
- *Taiheiki* (Chronicle of Great Peace) — partially trans.

## Key Academic Works

- Kenneth Grossberg — Muromachi shogunate studies
- H. Paul Varley, *Japanese Culture* (4th ed., 2000)
- Andrew Tuck — Muromachi cultural history
- Donald Keene — Japanese literature survey
- Thomas Kasulis — Zen aesthetics

## Needed

- Studies on the tally trade with Ming China
- Analysis of the Sengoku daimyo court patronage of arts
- Recent scholarship on the Ōnin War's causes and course
