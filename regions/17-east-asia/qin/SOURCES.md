# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Shiji* (Records of the Grand Historian), Sima Qian — chapters on Qin; trans. Burton Watson
- Qin legal texts from Shuihudi — "Qin Legal Texts" (Shuihudi Qin mu zhu jian); trans. A.F.P. Hulsewé
- *Shang Jun Shu* (Book of Lord Shang) — trans. J.J.L. Duyvendak or Yuri Pines
- *Han Feizi* — trans. W.K. Liao

## Key Academic Works

- Yuri Pines, *The Book of Lord Shang* (2017)
- Yuri Pines, *Envisioning Eternal Empire* (2009) — Qin-Han political thought
- Robin D.S. Yates — Qin military and legal texts
- Lothar von Falkenhausen — Qin material culture
- Michael Loewe and Edward Shaughnessy (eds.), *The Cambridge History of Ancient China* (1999)
