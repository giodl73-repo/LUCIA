# Sources — Melanesian Peoples (Fiji, Vanuatu, Solomons)

## Key Academic Works
- Roger Keesing & Peter Corris, *Lightning Meets the West Wind: The Malaita Massacre* (1980) — Solomons colonial violence
- Lamont Lindstrom, *Cargo Cult: Strange Stories of Desire from Melanesia and Beyond* (1993)
- Vanuatu Cultural Centre — kastom documentation and oral history archive (Port Vila)
- Patrick Kirch, *On the Road of the Winds* (2000) — Fiji's Lapita origins
- Nicholas Thomas, *Entangled Objects: Exchange, Material Culture, and Colonialism in the Pacific* (1991)
- Marshall Sahlins, *Islands of History* (1985) — Fiji; the encounter with Captain Cook and historical consciousness
- Dorothy Shineberg, *They Came for Sandalwood* (1967) — the sandalwood trade and its consequences

## Archaeological Sources
- Lapita sites in Vanuatu and Fiji — Bedford, Spriggs, Kirch excavations
- Teouma cemetery, Efate (Vanuatu) — oldest Lapita cemetery; genetic evidence
- Fiji Lapita sites — Sigatoka and Naitabale
