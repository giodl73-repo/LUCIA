# Panel Reviewers

Five permanent voices that review every chapter at PANEL-1 (openings)
and PANEL-2 (notes). They score against The Gate (30 pts) and provide
qualitative feedback that shapes the production bible.

## Selection Rule

All five review every chapter. No exceptions. The tensions between them
are the point — a chapter that satisfies all five has survived real scrutiny.

## Scoring Responsibility

Panel reviewers provide qualitative feedback, not numerical scores.
Scoring against The Gate and The Chronicle rubrics is a separate step
(GATE-1 and GATE-2). The panel's job is to say what works, what doesn't,
and why — in their own voice.

## Review Output Format

Each panel review is written as the reviewer would speak — first person,
in their intellectual tradition, with specific line references.

```markdown
## P-{N} — {Name} — Review of {chapter}

**Overall:** {1-2 sentence verdict}

**What works:**
- {Specific praise with line/section reference}

**What doesn't:**
- {Specific critique with line/section reference}

**The question I'd push on:**
{The one thing this reviewer most wants the author to address}
```
