**Note on clan name spellings:** "Epinayuu" appears in the scholarly literature also as "Epieyu" in older sources. The chapter uses "Epinayuu" consistently, consistent with the current standard orthography for Wayuunaiki.

**Note on ecological context:** The Guajira has a bimodal rainfall pattern: minor rains (April-May) and major rains (September-November). The chapter's references to "the wet season" use this simplified register for narrative clarity; the bimodal structure is the full ecological context for the pastoral migration patterns described.
