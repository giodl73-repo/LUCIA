# Sources — Indian Overseas

## Key Academic Works
- Brij Lal, *A Vision for Change: A.D. Patel and the Politics of Fiji* (1997) — the Indo-Fijian experience
- Madhavi Kale, *Fragments of Empire: Capital, Slavery, and Indian Indentured Labor Migration in the British Caribbean* (1998, Penn)
- Hugh Tinker, *A New System of Slavery: The Export of Indian Labour Overseas 1830–1920* (1974, Oxford) — the foundational account of indenture
- Vijay Prashad, *The Karma of Brown Folk* (2000, Minnesota) — the South Asian diaspora in the United States
- Maureen Swan, *Gandhi: The South African Experience* (1985) — Gandhi's 20 years and the development of satyagraha
- Gaiutra Bahadur, *Coolie Woman: The Odyssey of Indenture* (2013) — a personal and historical account of the girmit experience

## Primary Sources
- M.K. Gandhi, *The Story of My Experiments with Truth* (autobiography, 1925–1929)
- The Indian Indenture records — National Archives of India; Trinidad and Tobago National Archives
- The Ramcharitmanas (Tulsidas) — the primary sacred text of the North Indian Hindu diaspora
- The Anti-Indenture Campaign papers (1910–1917) — National Archives of India
