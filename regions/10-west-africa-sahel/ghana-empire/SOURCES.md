# Sources — Ghana Empire

## Primary

- Al-Bakri, *Kitab al-Masalik wa'l-Mamalik*, c. 1068 CE — the essential Arabic source; court ceremony, capital description
- Al-Idrisi, *Nuzhat al-Mushtaq*, c. 1154 CE — geographic account after the Ghana's decline
- Ibn Hawqal, *Surat al-Ard*, c. 977 CE — trade network description
- Ibn Khaldun, *Muqaddimah* and *Kitab al-Ibar* — later synthesis; Mali and Ghana in context

## Academic

- Levtzion, Nehemia. *Ancient Ghana and Mali*. 1973. Methuen.
- Mauny, Raymond. *Tableau géographique de l'Ouest africain au Moyen Age*. 1961. IFAN.
- Fage, J.D. *Ghana: A Historical Interpretation*. 1959. University of Wisconsin Press.
- Conrad, David C. & Fisher, Humphrey J. "The Conquest That Never Was: Ghana and the Almoravids, 1076." *History in Africa* 9 (1982). — revisionist argument on the Almoravid role
- Insoll, Timothy. *The Archaeology of Islam in Sub-Saharan Africa*. 2003. Cambridge University Press.
- Levtzion, Nehemia & Spaulding, Jay (eds.). *Medieval West Africa*. 2003. Markus Wiener.
