# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Amir Khusrau — Persian poetry and prose; *Khazain ul-Futuh*
- Ziauddin Barani, *Tarikh-i-Firuz Shahi* — court history of Alauddin Khilji and Firuz Shah
- Ibn Battuta, *Rihla* — North Africa/Middle East visitor's account of Muhammad bin Tughlaq's court
- *Tabaqat-i-Nasiri* (Minhaj-i-Siraj Juzjani) — early Sultanate history

## Key Academic Works

- Peter Jackson, *The Delhi Sultanate: A Political and Military History* (1999)
- Sunil Kumar, *The Emergence of the Delhi Sultanate* (2007)
- Romila Thapar, *Early India* (2002)
- Richard M. Eaton, *India in the Persianate Age* (2019)
- Cynthia Talbot — Hindu-Muslim frontier studies
