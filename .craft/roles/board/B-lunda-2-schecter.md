# B-lunda-2 — Robert Schecter

## Background

Robert Schecter's 1976 University of Wisconsin dissertation examined the
historiography of Lunda expansion — how the franchise model worked in practice
across the expanding territory of the Lunda state. His work provides the most
detailed analysis of how titled subordinates carried Lunda political culture
outward and how daughter states maintained connections to the center through
ceremonial rather than administrative means.

## Key Question

**"Is the franchise mechanism correctly characterized in its specifics —
what exactly did a titleholder carry, and how did it bind a daughter state
to the center?"**
