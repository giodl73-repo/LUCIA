# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Tausug oral traditions and genealogies (tarsilas)
- Spanish colonial records — the Moro Wars documentation
- British records — the 1878 Sabah agreement
- American military records — the Bud Dajo (1906) and Bud Bagsak (1913) campaigns

## Key Academic Works

- Thomas Kiefer, *The Tausug: Violence and Law in a Philippine Moslem Society* (1972)
- James Francis Warren, *The Sulu Zone, 1768–1898* (1981) — essential study of the slave-raiding economy
- James Francis Warren, *Iranun and Balangingi: Globalization, Maritime Raiding and the Birth of Ethnicity* (2002)
- Peter Gowing — Moro people and Islam in the Philippines
- Samuel Tan — Sulu and Bangsamoro history

## Needed

- Studies on the Sabah sovereignty dispute in international law
- Analysis of the Sulu maritime network at its height
- Recent scholarship on the Bangsamoro peace process and the legacy of the sultanate
