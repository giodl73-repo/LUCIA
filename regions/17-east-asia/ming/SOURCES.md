# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Ming Shi* (History of the Ming) — the official history
- Ma Huan, *Yingya Shenglan* (Overall Survey of the Ocean's Shores) — Zheng He's voyages; trans. J.V.G. Mills
- *Shuihu Zhuan* (Water Margin), *Sanguo Yanyi* (Three Kingdoms), *Xiyou Ji* (Journey to the West) — vernacular novels; multiple translations

## Key Academic Works

- Frederick Mote, *Imperial China 900–1800* (1999)
- Timothy Brook, *The Confusions of Pleasure: Commerce and Culture in Ming China* (1998)
- Edward Dreyer — Zheng He studies
- Evelyn Rawski — Ming-Qing studies
- Peter Ditmanson — Ming examination culture
