**P3 Flag (borderline):** "He was not, when he was born, important enough for anyone to record the precise location." — This sentence (in figures.md, Ahmad Shah entry) is close to textbook-critical in register. However, it functions as honest uncertainty (Style Principle #13) and is in figures.md where a slightly more reflective register is appropriate. Retain.

### Section 3 (Court and Chancery) — Voice: CONSISTENT
The chancery section correctly shifts to the layered, observational register of court culture. The ink preparation detail (oak gall, iron sulfate) is exactly the kind of method-level specificity that grounds the section. The bilingual movement between Pashto and Persian is described from inside the practice, not from outside.

**P2 Flag:** "The two languages did not produce two men. They produced a king who was twice legible."
This sentence is strong and belongs to the chapter's voice. However, "legible" is slightly literary-critical in register — a word from the vocabulary of textual analysis. It is not a textbook word but it carries a modern theoretical affect. The sentence earns its place; this is a P3 judgment call, not a P2. Retain with awareness.

### Section 4 (Panipat) — Voice: MINOR DRIFT
The tactical analysis in the Panipat section drifts slightly toward military-historical narrative register: "40,000 to 60,000 Maratha cavalry and infantry against 40,000 Durrani and allied forces." Numbers in this form carry the feeling of military history reference rather than inhabited narrative. The numbers are necessary but could be more embedded in the physical experience of the scene.

**P2 Flag:** The sentence "He was approximately eighteen to twenty years old" (in context of Vishwasrao's death passage) shifts briefly into reference mode — the hedge ("approximately") and the age-range together read as a note from sources rather than as prose.

**Recommendation:** Revise: "He was young — eighteen or twenty, the sources give different ages." This keeps the uncertainty while sounding like prose rather than a citation.

### Section 5 (Landing) — Voice: CONSISTENT AND STRONG
The landing returns to the opening's deliberate register with full force. The mulberry smoke image returning to close the chapter is exactly right. The voice is at its most assured here.
