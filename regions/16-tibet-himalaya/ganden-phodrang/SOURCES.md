# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Fifth Dalai Lama, *Secret Visions* and autobiography — trans. Kurtis Schaeffer
- Thirteenth Dalai Lama's political testament (1932)
- *Dzungar occupation records* — partially trans.
- Qing Ambans' reports from Lhasa — partially translated in Luciano Petech's work

## Key Academic Works

- Luciano Petech, *China and Tibet in the Early 18th Century* (1950)
- Melvyn Goldstein, *A History of Modern Tibet* (1989)
- Elliot Sperling — Tibetan-Chinese political relationship studies
- Kheme Sonam Wangdu and Hildegard Diemberger — Fifth Dalai Lama studies
- Per Kvaerne — Bon tradition in relationship to Buddhist Tibet

## Needed

- Recent scholarship on the Great Fifth's diplomatic activities
