# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Rekidai Hoan* (Precious Documents of Successive Generations) — Ryukyuan diplomatic records
- Great Bell of Shuri inscription (1458)
- Chinese tribute records (Ming and Qing Veritable Records sections on Ryukyu)

## Key Academic Works

- Gregory Smits, *Visions of Ryukyu: Identity and Ideology in Early-Modern Thought and Politics* (1999)
- George Kerr, *Okinawa: The History of an Island People* (1958, revised 2000) — still the standard
- Matthew Allen — Ryukyuan history and identity
- Koji Taira — Ryukyu-Okinawa social history
- Dana Masayuki — Ryukyuan trade networks
