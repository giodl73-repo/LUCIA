# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Zahir ud-Din Babur, *Baburnama* (c. 1530) — the essential Timurid autobiography; military campaigns and personal reflections
- Sharafuddin Ali Yazdi, *Zafarnama* (c. 1425) — panegyric biography of Timur
- Clavijo, *Embassy to Tamerlane* (1403–1406) — Castilian envoy's eyewitness account of Samarkand
- Timurid miniature manuscripts — the visual record of the Renaissance (Herat and Samarkand schools)

## Key Academic Works

- Beatrice Forbes Manz, *The Rise and Rule of Tamerlane* (1989) — essential political history
- Maria Eva Subtelny, *Timurids in Transition* (2007)
- Priscilla Soucek — Timurid art and architecture
- Thomas Lentz and Glenn Lowry, *Timur and the Princely Vision* (1989)
- Lisa Golombek and Donald Wilber, *The Timurid Architecture of Iran and Turan* (1988)
