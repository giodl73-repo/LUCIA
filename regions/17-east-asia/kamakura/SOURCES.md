# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Zeami Motokiyo, *Fushikaden* (Teachings on Style and the Flower) — trans. Rimer and Yamazaki
- Dōgen, *Shōbōgenzō* (Treasury of the True Dharma Eye) — trans. various
- *Heike Monogatari* (Tale of the Heike) — trans. Royall Tyler (Viking, 2012)
- *Azuma Kagami* (Mirror of the East) — Kamakura official record

## Key Academic Works

- Jeffrey Mass — Kamakura shogunate studies
- Jeffrey Mass (ed.), *Court and Bakufu in Japan* (1982)
- H. Paul Varley — Japanese culture and Kamakura Buddhism
- Thomas Kasulis — Zen philosophy
- Hideo Kishimoto — Kamakura religious transformation
