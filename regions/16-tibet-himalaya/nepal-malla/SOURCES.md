# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Malla-era copper plate grants and stone inscriptions — National Archives of Nepal
- *Gopalarajavamsavali* — medieval Nepali chronicle
- Malla court festival records (Indra Jatra, Kumari selection records)

## Key Academic Works

- Mary Slusser, *Nepal Mandala: A Cultural Study of the Kathmandu Valley* (2 vols., 1982)
- John Sanday — Bhaktapur restoration and architectural studies
- Niels Gutschow — Kathmandu Valley urban studies
- Alexander Von Rospatt — Svayambhunath and Buddhist sacred space
- Gert Matthijs Koot — Newari art history

## Needed
