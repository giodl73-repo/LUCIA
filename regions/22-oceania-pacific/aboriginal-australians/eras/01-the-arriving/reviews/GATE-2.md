**Innovations flagged:**
1. **Country-as-Integration-Pivot** (Progression): Where the San chapter used a single species (eland) as the integration point, this chapter uses Country itself. Country is simultaneously physical landscape, Dreaming body, source of law, medium of knowledge, and identity of its people. This is a new application of the integration-pivot innovation.
2. **Triple-Redundancy as Structural Engine** (Progression): The chapter identifies the mechanism of persistence: knowledge encoded in three media (song/landscape/kinship) simultaneously, so that the failure of any one is survivable. This explains 65,000 years of continuity without asserting inevitability.
3. **Dreaming-Time as Deep-Time Solution** (Honesty): The chapter resolves the deep-time uncertainty problem by adopting the Dreaming's own temporal logic rather than imposing archaeological chronology. "The Tjukurpa does not count years." This is an inside-voice honesty technique specific to cultures with non-linear temporal frameworks.
