## P-4 — Wade Davis — PANEL-2 Review (Notes)

**Focus:** Equal depth for the ecological epistemology. The trance dance gets the opening; tracking must get equivalent treatment in the chapter.

**What works in the plan:**
- Section 2 is dedicated to tracking as epistemology. Good.
- The plan uses Liebenberg's work, which is the strongest scholarly treatment of tracking as cognitive practice.
- The eland section (5) promises to show the integration of spiritual, ecological, and social knowledge. This is the chapter's most important intellectual claim.

**What needs work:**
- The tracking section must show the reasoning, not just the skill. It is not enough to say the tracker can identify an animal from its spoor. The chapter must show the chain of inference: the tracker sees a mark, forms a hypothesis about what made it and when, looks for confirming or disconfirming evidence, revises, and follows. This is the same cognitive structure as scientific reasoning, and it must be visible in the scene without ever using the word "scientific."
- Plant knowledge is currently absent from the section plan. The San's botanical knowledge — hundreds of species identified and classified by use, habitat, seasonality, preparation method — is a major component of the knowledge system. Consider integrating this into Section 2 or Section 4. A scene of gathering would do it: a woman identifying plants as she walks, the children with her learning by following.
- Water-finding is absent. In the Kalahari, the ability to find water — in tsamma melons, in tubers, in hidden sip-wells — is the knowledge that makes desert life possible. This is the most direct demonstration of the knowledge system's sophistication: you cannot live here without it.

**The structural point:**
The San's epistemology is not a collection of separate skills (tracking, plant knowledge, trance, rain-making). It is a single integrated way of knowing the world, where ecological observation, spiritual practice, and social obligation are aspects of one system. The chapter's arc must reveal this integration, not catalog its parts. The eland section is where this should happen, but seeds should be planted earlier.
