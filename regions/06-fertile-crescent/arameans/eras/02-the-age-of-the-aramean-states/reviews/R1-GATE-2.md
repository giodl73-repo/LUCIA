Each section earns the next. The terminal condition (Assyria adopts the language it cannot replace) is visible in the final paragraphs. |

Then returns to the gate of Dammasq from the opening (the messenger who now carries terms of submission, not trade). The final line: "The road did not close. It changed hands. And the language on the road kept moving." The culture's own persistence mechanism (the road and the language) delivers the final note. |
