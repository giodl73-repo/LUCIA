### Register Identification

The chapter operates in a mountain-spare register: deliberate, weight on the noun, colon and period as punctuation rather than em-dash, short declarative sentences for high-stakes moments. This is a new register distinct from steppe (relentless forward motion), saga-spare (Nordic understatement), and vertical (altitude-structured Inca). The mountain register calculates rather than narrates — the prose thinks as the highland lord thinks.
