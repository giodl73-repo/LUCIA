**2. P2 — Single sentence departure, "Before the Kingdom" section.**
"The *bertsolaritza* played its role here: the improvisational verse-maker who could summarize a decision, a boundary, an agreement in rhymed form that the community could remember and repeat. Not entertainment. An archive."

The sentence "Not entertainment. An archive." is the chapter voice — correct and strong. The preceding sentence, however, has a slightly more explanatory register than the baseline ("played its role here: the..."). It is telling the reader the bertsolar's function rather than showing the bertsolar functioning. This is minor — the showing happens later in the ferrería scene — but the sentence anticipates rather than demonstrates. Author may leave as-is (the later scene compensates) or revise to remove the anticipatory explanation.

**6. P3 (minor) — "This was Sancho's political genius" (Sancho III section).**
"This was Sancho's political genius: he had turned geography into theology." This sentence is summary/commentary rather than mountain-laconic showing. The characterization is earned by the preceding passage about the Camino, but the label "political genius" is the author naming the effect rather than leaving the reader to name it. The reader has already understood what Sancho did. The sentence could be cut or revised to show rather than characterize. Author's call — the passage is not a P1 violation (no forbidden framing) but it verges on the earned-emotion violation the Compression Editor will also flag.
