## Notes

**Gate: PASSED — 30/30.**

This is a near-perfect opening. The structural discovery here is remarkable: the reggae offbeat IS the prose's own argument structure. The opening performs the thesis — you don't read ABOUT rhythmic inversion, you experience it in the prose. The sentence "What the ear heard as a space, the music heard as a statement" is the chapter's governing principle stated at the level of form, not content.

Voice register: This is a new register candidate. It operates like the Prophetic-Migratory register (gathering momentum, prophetic certainty) but is organized around rhythmic inversion rather than migratory forward movement. The pause falls where the Western reader expects emphasis. Candidate for Innovation Log.

Em-dash count: 0. Voice discipline confirmed.

Innovations flagged for Stage 8 (GATE-2): rhythmic-inversion as structural prose principle (potentially a new Voice register discovery).
