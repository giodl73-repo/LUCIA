# Sources — Dutch Empire

## Key Academic Works
- Femme Gaastra, *The Dutch East India Company: Expansion and Decline* (2003, Walburg Pers)
- Jonathan Israel, *Dutch Primacy in World Trade, 1585–1740* (1989, Oxford)
- C.R. Boxer, *The Dutch Seaborne Empire 1600–1800* (1965, Hutchinson)
- Adam Smith, *The Wealth of Nations* (1776) — discusses the VOC as a case study in corporate colonialism
- Gijs Havik — Dutch Brazil; the WIC in Pernambuco
- Ulbe Bosma — the Cultuurstelsel and Javanese society

## Primary Sources
- Multatuli (Eduard Douwes Dekker), *Max Havelaar* (1860) — the anti-colonial exposé
- VOC records — Nationaal Archief, The Hague; the largest single colonial archive in existence
- Engelbert Kaempfer, *History of Japan* (1727)
- Jan Pieterszoon Coen's letters to the VOC directors (collected in VOC archives)
