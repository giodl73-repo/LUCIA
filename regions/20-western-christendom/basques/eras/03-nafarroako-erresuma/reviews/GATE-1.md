## Notes

**Passing score: 29/30. Gate cleared.**

Strong dimension: Voice. The mountain-laconic register is discovered, not borrowed — no equivalent in the confirmed voice spectrum. Deliberate, spare, each sentence placed rather than flowing. Distinct from Saga-Spare (which records without emotion) in that this voice holds within its brevity the weight of deep time and pre-literate claim.

Minor texture note: the gorge itself could carry one more physical anchor — the sound of the rearguard, the clatter of armor, the particular silence after the boulders stop — but this is a refinement, not a failure. 4/5 is fully earned.

**Honesty score of 5 confirmed:** "This is not a complaint. It is simply the record" is an exact instance of the v1.2 No-Verdict Decline via Cultural Attribution technique. The refusal to judge the Chanson de Roland is attributed to the Basque way of keeping the record, not to editorial restraint. The honesty belongs to the voice.

**Innovation candidate:** The framing of the poem's erasure as evidence about the poem rather than injustice to the Basques ("the Basques, who had actually won at Orreaga, do not appear in it") may qualify as a new application of the v1.2 Outsider's Bias as Inside Characterization technique — extended from chronicle-accounts to literary monuments. Flag for INNOVATIONS.md at FINAL stage.
