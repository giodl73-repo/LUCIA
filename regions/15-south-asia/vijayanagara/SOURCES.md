# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Domingo Paes, *Narrative of Domingo Paes* (c. 1520) — Portuguese merchant's account
- Abdur Razzaq, *Matla-us-Sadain* (1442) — Persian ambassador's description of Vijayanagara
- Krishnadevaraya, *Amuktamalyada* — Telugu poem; trans. Srinivas Reddy (2011)
- *Rayavacakamu* — Telugu court chronicle
- *Nagarakertagama* — Javanese poem (Majapahit court, relevant for context)

## Key Academic Works

- Burton Stein, *Vijayanagara* (1989, New Cambridge History of India)
- Philip Wagoner — Vijayanagara and Islamic court culture synthesis
- Cynthia Talbot, *Precolonial India in Practice* (2001)
- Anila Verghese — temple patronage and iconography
- George Michell, *Vijayanagara: The City of Victory* (1995)
