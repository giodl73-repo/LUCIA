## Voice Registry Note

**Foral-Mountain register** identified and confirmed at opening and chapter length. Characteristics:
- Short declarative sentences with the weight of stone
- Legal-weight endings that close like a verdict
- Sentences placed rather than flowing — each one a foothold
- Sensory grounding where the material world is present (pass, forge, oak)
- Zero em-dashes as emergent property of the register
- The analytical argument carried in the same register as the narrative — the *foru* mechanism explained in the same voice as the ambush

Distinct from: Saga-Spare (cooler, less assertive, more laconic), Legal-Public (more institutional, less communal), Oral-Tradition (more performative, less declarative).

Recommend adding to STYLE-GUIDE.md Voice Spectrum when confirmed in a second Basque-tradition chapter or a closely analogous mountain-covenant culture.
