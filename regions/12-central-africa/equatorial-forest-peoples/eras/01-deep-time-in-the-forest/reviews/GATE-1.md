## Notes

The Gate is passed. Score: 29/30. Target: 24+/30.

One minor adjustment worth noting for the chapter: the brief shift to analytical enumeration ("ecological and positional and relational and embodied all at once") edges toward an outside-observer frame. In the chapter, this conceptual content should stay embedded in scene and action. The botanical/ecological knowledge list (forty-three tree species, caterpillar emergence, bark medicine) is excellent evidence — needs to stay at method level (how the knowledge works, not what it is) in the chapter expansion.

Voice finding: this is an "Observational-Intimate" register — distinct from the griot cadence, from maritime accumulative, from oral tradition cadence. Closest to the Aboriginal/San oral-tradition voice but quieter, more sensory. The forest's density and complexity creates a voice that notices everything without announcing what it notices. This should be the register for the full chapter.
