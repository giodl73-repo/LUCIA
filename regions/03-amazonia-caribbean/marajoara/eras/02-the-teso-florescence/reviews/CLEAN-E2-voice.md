This phrase does not appear in the chapter but is a risk in the chiefdom/heterarchy passage (Section 3). The passage that reads: "The question is not fully answered. The mitigation: the sentence moves quickly from the scholarly citation to the material evidence, and the material evidence is described in the chapter's own register. Acceptable.

This phrase does not appear in the chapter text. The notes discussed this debate; the chapter itself elected not to mention it by name. This is the correct decision. The chapter presents the material evidence without naming the scholarly categories. Voice is maintained.
