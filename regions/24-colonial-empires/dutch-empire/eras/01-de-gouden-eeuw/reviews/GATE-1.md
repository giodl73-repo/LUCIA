## Notes

**Em-dash count:** 0. The Counting-House register found its own pause mechanism (the period, the colon, the comma-chain) without reaching for the dash. This is the correct symptom of voice discipline.

**Word count:** ~820 words. Slightly over the ~800 target but within acceptable range given the structural necessity of the final two sentences as a landing device.

**Voice identification:** The Counting-House register (validated: Italian City-States, *La Rivoluzione Commerciale*) applies without distortion. The Dutch VOC is precisely the culture for which this register was designed: partnership-accountable commerce, ledger-precise, unafraid of numbers. The adaptation required: the ledger entry as framing device (opening and closing); the chain-of-distance as the analytical engine replacing the commenda / bill-of-exchange mechanics of the Italian instance.

**Structural engine:** The opening plants the engine clearly — corporate distance from consequence, the subscription table as the designed endpoint of a chain that begins eleven thousand kilometers east. This is not metaphor; it is the VOC's actual institutional logic, and it will drive the chapter's arc.

**The Achebe Test:** A thoughtful Bandanese scholar reading this opening would find their people present, not erased — the chain of inference is named, the violence is named, the deliberate design of the distance is named. A thoughtful Dutch corporate historian would recognize the Heeren XVII governance model, the subscription mechanics, and the operational logic. A thoughtful descendant of Pieter Bas the baker would recognize the ordinary citizen drawn into a system whose consequences he could not see. All three readings are available simultaneously. The test passes.
