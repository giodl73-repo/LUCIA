# Sources — Castile and León

## Primary Sources

- *Cantar de mio Cid* — c. 1200; the epic poem; the foundational text of Castilian literature
- *Crónica General* — Alfonso X's prose chronicle of Spain; 13th century
- *Siete Partidas* — Alfonso X's comprehensive law code; 1265; the most ambitious medieval legal compilation in the vernacular
- *Poema de Fernán González* — 10th-century count of Castile; the formation of Castilian identity
- Rodrigo Jiménez de Rada, *Historia de rebus Hispanie* — 13th-century Archbishop of Toledo's Latin history
- The Toledo translation texts — Gerard of Cremona's translations and those of other Toledo school scholars
- *Las Navas de Tolosa* letters — contemporary accounts of the 1212 battle
