# Sources — Lunda

## Primary

- Lacerda e Almeida, *Travessia de Africa* (1798–1799) — Portuguese governor's account of the attempt to reach Kazembe; died on the journey; journal published posthumously
- Livingstone, David. *Last Journals* — encounters with Lunda-area states in the 1860s–1870s
- Cameron, V.L. — nineteenth century traveler accounts of the Mwaant Yav's court

## Academic

- Vansina, Jan. *Kingdoms of the Savanna*. 1966. University of Wisconsin Press.
- Schecter, Robert. *History and Historiography on a Frontier of Lunda Expansion*. PhD dissertation, University of Wisconsin, 1976.
- Papstein, Robert. "The Upper Zambezi: A History of the Luvale People 1000–1900." PhD dissertation, UCLA, 1978. — daughter state perspective
- Miller, Joseph C. *Kings and Kinsmen: Early Mbundu States in Angola*. 1976. Clarendon Press. — western Lunda connections
- Pritchett, James A. *The Lunda-Ndembu: Style, Change, and Social Transformation in South Central Africa*. 2001. University of Wisconsin Press.
- Birmingham, David. *Trade and Conflict in Angola: The Mbundu and Their Neighbours Under the Influence of the Portuguese 1483–1790*. 1966. Oxford University Press.
