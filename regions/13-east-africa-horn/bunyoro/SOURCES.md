# Sources — Bunyoro / Kitara

## Primary / Oral

- Bunyoro royal traditions — maintained by court historians; the Bito dynasty chronicles
- John Nyakatura, *Anatomy of an African Kingdom: A History of Bunyoro-Kitara* (trans. Teopista Muganwa, 1973) — the foundational inside account
- Bachwezi shrine traditions — maintained by medium specialists; oral theological tradition

## Academic

- Uzoigwe, G.N. *Revolution and Revolt in Bunyoro-Kitara*. 1970. Longman Uganda.
- Beattie, John. *The Nyoro State*. 1971. Oxford University Press. — the political structure of Bunyoro
- Doyle, Shane. *Crisis and Decline in Bunyoro: Population and Environment in Western Uganda 1860–1955*. 2006. James Currey.
- Vansina, Jan. *Antecedents to Modern Rwanda: The Nyiginya Kingdom*. 2004. University of Wisconsin Press. — regional comparative context
- Posnansky, Merrick. "Bigo bya Mugenyi." *Uganda Journal* 33:2 (1969). — the Bigo earthworks
- Sutton, J.E.G. "The Antecedents of the Interlacustrine Kingdoms." *Journal of African History* 34:1 (1993).
