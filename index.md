# LUCIA — The Human Chronicle

The story of every human culture, told from inside their own worldview.
Each chapter opens with a real person in a real moment — context emerges from narrative.

---

## The 5-Level Tree

```
L1  Region
  └── L2  People
        └── L3  Era
              ├── L4  Files
              │    ├── opening.md
              │    ├── chapter.md
              │    ├── notes.md
              │    └── figures.md
              │
              └── L5  Reviews
                   ├── R1-*.md
                   ├── R2-*.md
                   └── BOARD-*.md
```

26 regions. ~250-300 peoples. Thousands of eras.
