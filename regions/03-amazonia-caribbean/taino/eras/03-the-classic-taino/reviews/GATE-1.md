## Notes

This opening achieves a Gate score of 30/30. Every dimension lands at its highest anchor.

The v1.2 Honesty amendment is operating at its most sophisticated: the text does not refuse to acknowledge the coming catastrophe — it inhabits the Taíno epistemic condition (you work for a world that will outlast you; you cannot know by how much) and lets that condition do the ethical work. The foreknowledge is present, but it belongs to the chapter's moral framework, not to an outside observer's tragedy-framing.

The areíto-cadence voice is new to the Voice Spectrum. It deserves logging as a potential innovation: **Areíto-Ceremonial** — cumulative parallel construction with period-stops, the repetition structure of oral transmission as the prose's own rhythm. Carries both analytical and narrative weight in the same register.

The sensory-analytical fusion (v1.4 Style Guide amendment) is operating: "The words are not separate from the motion" does analytical work (areíto as integrated knowledge system) in the same gesture as sensory rendering.
