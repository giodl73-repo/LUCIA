# Sources — Norman Kingdoms

## Primary Sources

- William of Jumièges, *Gesta Normannorum Ducum* — the Norman ducal history; multiple versions 11th–12th century
- William of Poitiers, *Gesta Guillelmi* — biography of William the Conqueror; contemporary, partisan
- *Domesday Book* (1086) — the great survey; Winchester and Exeter volumes
- *Bayeux Tapestry* — c. 1070s; the visual narrative of the Conquest; made in Canterbury
- Orderic Vitalis, *Historia Ecclesiastica* — 12th century; the fullest Norman chronicle; Orderic was Anglo-Norman himself
- Roger of Hoveden, *Chronica* — English chronicle continuing Norman administrative history
- Hugo Falcandus, *Historia de Rebus in Sicilia* — 12th century; the Norman court in Sicily from inside
- *Liber de Regno Siciliae* — administrative records of the Norman kingdom of Sicily
