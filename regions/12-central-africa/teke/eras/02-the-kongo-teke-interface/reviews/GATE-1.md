## Notes

This opening achieves the full score in all six dimensions. The voice register — transactional, calculating, world-weary — is both distinctive and inseparable from the story being told. The broker's interior calculation is rendered without being explained; the reader understands the commercial logic of the Teke position from the inside, not from a lecture about it.

The engine is already visible: the broker's power is structural (standing in the middle, knowing both ends), and the terminal condition will derive from how that structure changes when the Atlantic trade reorders the coastal end. That is the chapter's arc.
