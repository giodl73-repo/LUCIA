# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Joseon Wangjo Sillok* (Annals of the Joseon Dynasty) — 1,893 volumes; searchable database at sillok.history.go.kr
- King Sejong's proclamation of Hunminjeongeum (1443) — hangul announcement
- Jeong Yakyong (Dasan), *Mongmin simseo* (Admonitions on Governing the People) — trans. various
- Yi Sunsin, *Nanjung ilgi* (War Diary) — trans. Ha Tae-hung

## Key Academic Works

- Ki-baik Lee, *A New History of Korea* (1984)
- JaHyun Kim Haboush — Joseon cultural history, especially court women
- Michael Kalton — Neo-Confucianism in Korea
- Eugene Park — Joseon social history
- Samuel Hawley, *The Imjin War* (2005) — the Japanese invasions
