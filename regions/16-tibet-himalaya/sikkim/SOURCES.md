# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Sikkimese royal chronicles (*Lho-Mon-Tsong-Sum*) — partially translated
- Darjeeling cession records (1835) — British India Office
- Anglo-Chinese Convention on Sikkim (1890)
- Palden Thondup Namgyal's writings and correspondence

## Key Academic Works

- Andrew Duff, *Sikkim: Requiem for a Himalayan Kingdom* (2015)
- Melvyn Goldstein — Himalayan kingdom studies
- Indra Bahadur Rai — Nepali-language Sikkim history
- Leo Rose, *The Politics of Bhutan* (1977) — comparative Himalayan kingdoms
