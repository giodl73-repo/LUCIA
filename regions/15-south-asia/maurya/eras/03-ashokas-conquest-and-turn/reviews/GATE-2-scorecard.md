## Innovations Flagged

1. **Edict-as-Voice (Voice dimension):** Opening with the primary source text itself as the chapter's first lines, then pulling back to show the act of inscription. The edict IS the voice. The culture's own written medium becomes the narrator's register. This is distinct from previous voice discoveries (oral tradition cadence, steppe rhythm, court layering) because it uses the physical medium of inscription as the structural principle of the prose. The declarative, weighted, enumerative quality of the rock edict becomes the chapter's own sentence rhythm.

2. **Stone Paradox as Structural Engine (Progression dimension):** The paradox that the medium of sovereignty (carved stone) becomes the medium of vulnerability (carved grief), and that the vulnerability proves more durable than the power, is a structural engine that drives the arc from conquest to landing. Similar to but distinct from the self-consuming redistribution engine (Mali) or the grief-violence cycle (Haudenosaunee).
