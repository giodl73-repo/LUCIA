# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Licchavi stone inscriptions — Dhanavajra Vajracharya, *Lichhavikalka Abhilekha* (Nepali)
- Changu Narayan inscription (464 CE) — earliest dated inscription in Nepal
- Harigaon inscription records

## Key Academic Works

- Dhanavajra Vajracharya — Licchavi inscription studies (primary Nepali scholarship)
- Luciano Petech, *Medieval History of Nepal* (1984)
- Mary Slusser, *Nepal Mandala: A Cultural Study of the Kathmandu Valley* (2 vols., 1982) — comprehensive
- Siegfried Lienhard — Newari art and early Nepalese history
- John Whelpton, *A History of Nepal* (2005)
