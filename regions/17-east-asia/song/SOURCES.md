# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Su Shi (Su Dongpo), Ouyang Xiu, Li Qingzhao — poetry and prose; various translations
- *Dongjing Menghua Lu* (Dreams of Splendor of the Eastern Capital, Meng Yuanlao) — Song Kaifeng memoir
- *Zizhi Tongjian* — Song chapters
- Wang Anshi's reform memorials — partially trans.

## Key Academic Works

- Robert Hartwell — Song economic history (foundational articles)
- Dieter Kuhn, *The Age of Confucian Rule: The Song Transformation of China* (2009)
- Peter Bol, *This Culture of Ours: Intellectual Transitions in T'ang and Sung China* (1992)
- Robert Hymes and Conrad Schirokauer (eds.), *Ordering the World* (1993)
- Richard von Glahn — Song monetary history
