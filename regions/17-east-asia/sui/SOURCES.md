# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Wei Zheng et al., *Sui Shu* (History of the Sui) — the official history; partially trans.
- *Zizhi Tongjian* (Comprehensive Mirror in Aid of Governance), Sima Guang — Sui chapters; trans. Rafe de Crespigny for adjacent periods

## Key Academic Works

- Arthur Wright, *The Sui Dynasty* (1978) — foundational
- David Graff, *Medieval Chinese Warfare, 300–900* (2002)
- Mark Edward Lewis, *China Between Empires: The Northern and Southern Dynasties* (2009)
- Howard Wechsler — Sui-Tang transition studies

## Needed
