# Sources — Mutapa

## Primary

- Portuguese state records — Estado da India archives; the treaty texts and correspondence
- Dominican missionary reports — seventeenth century; the court Christianity documentation
- João dos Santos, *Ethiopia Oriental* (1609) — the most detailed Portuguese account of the Mutapa court

## Academic

- Beach, D.N. *The Shona and Zimbabwe 900–1850*. 1980. Heinemann.
- Mudenge, S.I.G. *A Political History of Munhumutapa c.1400–1902*. 1988. Zimbabwe Publishing House. — the definitive political history
- Newitt, Malyn. *Portuguese Settlement on the Zambesi*. 1973. Longman. — the Portuguese penetration
- Pikirayi, Innocent. *The Zimbabwe Culture*. 2001. AltaMira Press. — archaeological context
- Bhila, H.H.K. *Trade and Politics in a Shona Kingdom: The Manyika and Their African and Portuguese Neighbours, 1575–1902*. 1982. Longman.

## Needed (ACQUISITION)

- Recent scholarship on the prazo system and its social effects on Shona populations
- Studies on the Dominican mission and court Christianity at the Mutapa court
- Analysis of the Changamire expulsion of the Portuguese (1680s-1690s)
- Archaeological surveys of Mutapa capital sites
