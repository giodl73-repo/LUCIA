# Sources — Old Kingdom Egypt

## Primary / Archaeological

- Pyramid Texts — Fifth and Sixth Dynasty pyramid interiors; oldest religious corpus in the world
- Giza plateau records — ongoing excavations by AERA (Ancient Egypt Research Associates); worker village, administrative records
- Palermo Stone — fragmentary annals of the early dynasties
- Mastaba and tomb inscriptions — Saqqara, Giza; biographical texts of officials

## Academic

- Lehner, Mark. *The Complete Pyramids*. 1997. Thames & Hudson. — architectural and organizational analysis
- Hawass, Zahi & Lehner, Mark. *Giza and the Pyramids*. 2017. Thames & Hudson. — comprehensive survey
- Kemp, Barry. *Ancient Egypt: Anatomy of a Civilization*. 3rd ed. 2018. Routledge. — social and economic structures
- Allen, James P. *The Ancient Egyptian Pyramid Texts*. 2005. Society of Biblical Literature. — translation and commentary
- Baud, Michel. *Djéser et la IIIe Dynastie*. 2002. Pygmalion. — Third Dynasty political history
- Strudwick, Nigel. *Texts from the Pyramid Age*. 2005. Society of Biblical Literature. — administrative and biographical texts
