## E-1 — Judgment Auditor

*Hunts outside-lens violations. Forbidden words. Anachronistic morality. Romanticization or demonization.*

**Forbidden words check:** Clear. No instances of primitive, advanced, tribe, Dark Ages, "discovered by," or "the New World." No comparative framings positioning Sassanid against a Western default.

**Outside-lens check:**

Passage flagged: "His religion spread along the Silk Road, west into North Africa (where Augustine would briefly convert to it), east into Central Asia and China and ultimately into the Uyghur Empire. The empire that tried to extinguish it had built the roads it traveled."

This passes the Achebe test, but the mention of Augustine requires care. Augustine is introduced as an external data point to demonstrate the spread of Manichaeism — he is evidence about the religion's reach, not evidence about Western significance. The framing keeps the focus on Mani's tradition, not on Augustine's. Acceptable as written. The parenthetical is genuinely informational rather than prestige-lending.

**Romanticization check:**

The sacred fires are described with reverence, but it is internal reverence — the reverence of the tradition itself, not the author's nostalgia for a lost practice. "The druj does not wait for kings who will not fight it" is in the tradition's own idiom, not an authorial endorsement. The Artabanus figures.md entry gives him genuine dignity without romanticizing the Arsacid period. The Mani entry in figures.md notes that "history does not always cooperate with the suppression orders carved in stone" — this is slightly wry, but it is a factual observation about how history worked, not a verdict on who was right. Acceptable.

**Anachronistic morality check:**

The chapter takes no position on whether Kartir's suppression of other religions was right or wrong. The inscription records both Shapur's grants to diverse communities and Kartir's suppression. The chapter presents the tension without resolving it. No anachronistic verdict entered.

**E-1 Result:** Two minor advisory notes, no required changes. Chapter passes judgment audit.

## E-2 — Voice Keeper

*Hunts register drift. Textbook voice. Modern commentary. Wrong audience.*

**Voice register:** Edict-Inscriptional throughout. Confirmed by paragraph-isolation test on three random selections:

*Test 1 (Section 2, fire temple):* "This is not ceremony. This is maintenance." — Inscriptional, declarative, no register drift.

*Test 2 (Section 4, Roman wars):* "The arch doesn't need to know whose river it crosses. He built the best bridge he knew how to build. Shapur rode across it. The bridge outlasted them both." — Kinetic modulation within inscriptional family. No drift.

*Test 3 (Section 5, inscription):* "The formula is precise. The invocation of divine origin is not metaphor. Shapur is not saying he was born of supernatural parents..." — This paragraph edges into explaining. The phrase "This is not inconsistency" in the following paragraph names the structural tension rather than letting the stone hold it. Flagged.

**Required revision (E-2):**

In Section 5, revise: *"This is not inconsistency. This is the structural tension of the founding: a state whose divine legitimacy required exclusivity and whose imperial reach required diversity, holding both at once, committing both to the permanent record in the same medium at the same site."*

To: *"The stone preserved both. The tension between them is the founding era's deepest legacy: a state whose divine legitimacy required exclusivity and whose imperial reach required diversity, holding both at once, committing both to the permanent record in the same medium at the same site."*

This removes the meta-comment ("This is not inconsistency") and lets the observation stand in the inscriptional voice.

Also flag from GATE-2 notes: "This is the claim the founders made. They carved it where it could be read from the road." In the landing, revise to: "They carved it where it could be read from the road." — remove "This is the claim the founders made" as authorial summary.

**Em-dash count:** 0. Confirmed.

**E-2 Result:** Two required revisions. Both minor, both voice-related, both easily corrected without structural change.
