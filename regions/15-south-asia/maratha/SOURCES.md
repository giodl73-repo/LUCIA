# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Sabhasad Bakhar* — Marathi chronicle of Shivaji's court
- Shivaji's letters (Shivaji's correspondence in Marathi and Persian)
- James Grant Duff, *History of the Mahrattas* (1826) — early colonial-era account, useful primary material
- Peshwa Daftar — administrative records from Pune; partially published

## Key Academic Works

- Stewart Gordon, *The Marathas 1600–1818* (1993, New Cambridge History of India)
- Jadunath Sarkar, *House of Shivaji* (1940) — classic study
- André Wink, *Land and Sovereignty in India* (1986)
- Prachi Deshpande — Maratha historical consciousness
- Richard M. Eaton, *India in the Persianate Age* (2019)

## Needed

- Recent Marathi-language scholarship on the confederacy period
