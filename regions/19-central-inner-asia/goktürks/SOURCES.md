# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Orkhon inscriptions (Bilge Khagan stele, Kül Tigin stele, Tonyukuk stele — early 8th century CE)
- Chinese dynastic records: *Sui shu*, *Tang shu* (Old and New Tang History)
- Byzantine diplomatic records — Göktürk embassies to Constantinople
- Sogdian merchant letters — commercial correspondence mentioning Türkic political conditions

## Key Academic Works

- Denis Sinor, ed., *The Cambridge History of Early Inner Asia* (1990) — essential reference
- Peter Golden, *An Introduction to the History of the Turkic Peoples* (1992)
- Christopher Beckwith, *Empires of the Silk Road* (2009)
- Étienne de la Vaissière — Sogdians and the Türkic khaganate
- Talat Tekin — Orkhon Türkic language and inscription analysis
