# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Du Fu, Li Bai, Wang Wei, Bai Juyi — poems; various anthologies; Stephen Owen's *The Poetry of the Early T'ang* (1977) and *The Great Age of Chinese Poetry* (1981)
- Xuanzang, *Datang Xiyu Ji* (Great Tang Records on the Western Regions) — trans. Samuel Beal
- *Zizhi Tongjian* — Tang chapters; partial trans. various

## Key Academic Works

- Denis Twitchett (ed.), *The Cambridge History of China, Volume 3: Sui and T'ang China* (1979)
- Denis Twitchett, *Writing and Official History under the T'ang* (1992)
- Mark Edward Lewis, *China's Cosmopolitan Empire: The Tang Dynasty* (2009)
- Edward Schafer, *The Golden Peaches of Samarkand* (1963) — Tang material culture from Central Asia
- Susan Whitfield, *Life Along the Silk Road* (1999)
