# Sources — Axum

## Primary / Archaeological

- Axumite inscriptions — Ezana's trilingual inscriptions (Ge'ez, South Arabian, Greek); the military and religious records
- Periplus of the Erythraean Sea — first century CE; the Adulis port and Axumite commercial system
- Cosmas Indicopleustes, *Christian Topography*, c. 550 CE — Greek merchant's account of Axum and the Red Sea
- Axum archaeological complex — British Institute in Eastern Africa; Stelae Park excavations

## Academic

- Munro-Hay, Stuart. *Aksum: An African Civilization of Late Antiquity*. 1991. Edinburgh University Press. — the comprehensive reference
- Phillipson, David W. *Foundations of an African Civilisation: Aksum and the Northern Horn, 1000 BC–AD 1300*. 2012. James Currey.
- Phillipson, David W. *Ancient Ethiopia: Aksum, Its Antecedents and Successors*. 1998. British Museum Press.
- Kobishchanov, Y.M. *Axum*. 1979. Pennsylvania State University Press. — Russian scholarly tradition
- Pankhurst, Richard. *The Ethiopians: A History*. 1998. Blackwell.
