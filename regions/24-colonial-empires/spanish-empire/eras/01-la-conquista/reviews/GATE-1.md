## Notes

This opening achieves a near-perfect score across all six dimensions. The structural discovery is significant: the Requerimiento is not merely the hook but the engine — a document that is simultaneously law, theology, performance, and self-indictment. This functions as a **Material Object as Structural Principle** (v1.5 rubric) in the sense that the document's own properties determine the narrative's logic: the Requerimiento IS the Spanish Empire's self-understanding in concentrated form.

**Voice register:** Legal-Theological, adapted. The register discovered here is distinct from the Hammurabi Legal-Theological in that it holds simultaneously the sincere believer (Palacios Rubios), the devastated witness (Las Casas), and the institutional form (the Crown's documentation) — all within the same theological framework, none of them from outside it. This is a new modulation of the Legal-Theological register worth watching.

**Honesty observation:** The "laughed" moment (Las Casas) is handled exceptionally. His laughter is his, not the text's. The text does not endorse the laughter or explain it to the reader. This is v1.2 Inside-Voice Honesty operating at the opening level.
