## Innovations Flagged

**Potential innovation — Commercial Discretion as Structural Silence:** The chapter uses the broker's professional requirement not to write things down as a double technique: it explains the source gap (why the Teke record is thin) AND characterizes the culture (information is the resource that cannot be given away). This is a variant of the v1.5 "source gap as cultural honesty" technique but applied specifically to commercial cultures where non-documentation is a deliberate strategic choice rather than a technical limitation. The broker does not write because writing would be a commercial error. This may warrant logging in INNOVATIONS.md as an extension of the v1.5 technique — "Strategic Non-Documentation as Cultural Voice."
