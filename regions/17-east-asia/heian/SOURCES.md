# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Murasaki Shikibu, *Genji Monogatari* (Tale of Genji) — trans. Royall Tyler (Viking, 2001) or Edward Seidensticker
- Sei Shōnagon, *Makura no Sōshi* (The Pillow Book) — trans. Ivan Morris (Columbia, 1967)
- Murasaki Shikibu, *Diary* — trans. Richard Bowring
- Ki no Tsurayuki, *Tosa Nikki* (Tosa Diary) — trans. Helen McCullough

## Key Academic Works

- Ivan Morris, *The World of the Shining Prince* (1964) — Heian court culture
- H. Paul Varley, *Japanese Culture* (4th ed., 2000)
- William McCullough and Helen Craig McCullough — Heian literature translations
- Thomas Harper and Haruo Shirane (eds.), *Early Modern Japanese Literature* (Columbia, 2002)
- Royall Tyler — Genji scholarship
