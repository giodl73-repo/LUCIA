**Voice discovery:** This opening has discovered a new register not previously in the Voice Spectrum. I am calling it **Vodou-Revolutionary** provisionally:

- **Primary mechanism:** The ceremony's structure (invocation, possession, response, covenant) becomes the prose's organizing rhythm. The lwa's answer arrives in a short declarative that holds the weight of the longer sentence that preceded it. "The lwa answered through Cécile Fatiman." This is Call-and-Response at the paragraph level, not the sentence level.
- **Secondary mechanism:** The Scholarly-Revolutionary voice (from Fulani/Dan Fodio) contributes the mobilization register — the network, the runners, the structure beneath the surface. But where Scholarly-Revolutionary is a distributed correspondence becoming a governing order, Vodou-Revolutionary is a covenant becoming an army.
- **Pause mechanism:** The short declarative sentence that arrives after a longer building sentence, carrying the weight of both. Not a period-after-steppe-sentence (those are bare, percussive). This period is a held breath before the covenant lands.
- **Distinction from Call-and-Response (African Diaspora, Middle Passage):** The Middle Passage register is collective survival — what the trade could not confiscate. Vodou-Revolutionary is the inverse: collective action, the reappropriation of the confiscated. It is Call-and-Response mobilized.

This should be flagged to INNOVATIONS.md after GATE-2 if the register sustains through the full chapter.
