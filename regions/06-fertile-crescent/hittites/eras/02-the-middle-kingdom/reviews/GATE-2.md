**Notes:** Strong chapter. The structural engine operates throughout: every section is an examination of whether the written document can stop the killing, approached from a different angle (the blood it was written to stop, the mechanism it uses, the institution it empowers, the kingdom it serves, the aftermath it cannot control, the physical survival that outlasts everything). The voice is consistent and distinctive. The honest-uncertainty handling of the post-Telipinu period is a strength, not a weakness: the chapter does not claim more than the sources support.

**Innovations:** No new innovations to flag. The chapter executes existing techniques (structural engine, honest-limits figures, knowledge-system-as-evidence, sensory-analytical fusion) at a high level within a new register (treaty/edict voice). The register itself was already cataloged in v1.4 as a confirmed type (legal-public, adjacent to edict-inscriptional). The Hittite edict voice may warrant its own sub-designation if it appears at this level in subsequent chapters (Eras 1, 3, 4).
