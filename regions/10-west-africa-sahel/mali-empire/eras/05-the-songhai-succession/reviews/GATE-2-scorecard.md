## Innovations Flagged

**1. The Unchanging Song as Narrative Measure.** The chapter uses the jelilu's unchanging genealogy as the measure against which the empire's contraction becomes visible. The genealogy does not shorten when the empire does; this gap between the song and the reality it describes IS the chapter's central tension. The technique inverts the usual relationship between narrative and context: the context changes, the narrative stays the same, and the reader measures the change by the growing distance between the two. This is a structural technique the rubric does not yet anticipate under Progression.

**2. Recursive Structural Engine.** The chapter takes the structural engine innovation from Era 3 (the Kurukan Fuga as self-consuming mechanism) and traces it across two empires. The Songhai follow the same arc: concentration of power, succession conflict, contraction. The chapter makes this recursion visible without claiming it was inevitable, satisfying No Verdicts while delivering genuine structural insight. This extends the v1.1 Progression amendment by showing a structural engine operating across multiple chapters of the same culture's story.

**3. No-Verdict Decline.** The chapter successfully tells a story of political contraction without mourning, retrodiction, or judgment. "This was not a fall. The jelilu would not use the word." The technique attributes the refusal to judge to the culture's own voice, making No Verdicts an inside-voice discipline rather than an external editorial constraint. This is a significant craft achievement that the rubric's Honesty dimension does not yet specifically measure.

## Overall Assessment

This chapter succeeds at the hardest thing the Mali sequence asks: telling the story of contraction without verdict, while making the persistence of cultural institutions the climax rather than the consolation. The structural engine (the Kurukan Fuga's self-consuming redistribution logic) drives the arc from Era 3 to its terminal expression with a clarity that satisfies both narrative and analytical demands. The landing resonates across all five Mali eras. The main constraints are the thinner historical record, which limits figural depth, and occasional moments where the structural analysis approaches thesis-statement territory. The combined score of 81/90 represents the highest score in the project.
