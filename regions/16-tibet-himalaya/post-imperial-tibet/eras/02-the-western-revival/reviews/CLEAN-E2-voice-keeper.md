"The investment was calculated" is an author's frame, not the culture's voice. The information is necessary but the register dips into political-economic analysis mode. **P2: Single sentence departure. 2. **Section III, paragraph 4:** "Monasteries that received Guge's patronage owed Guge their allegiance. This reads as political analysis from outside, describing a patronage network in terms a political scientist might use. The content is sound but the register is analytical rather than devotional. **P2: Two sentences of register shift. Suggest reframing through the dharmaraja framework — the patronage as fulfilling the king's Buddhist obligation rather than as strategic networking.**
