# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Oracle bone inscriptions — Shang dynasty divination records; corpus of ~150,000 inscribed pieces
- Keightley, David N. — oracle bone translations and analysis (Harvard and Berkeley)
- Bronze vessel inscriptions (Western Zhou but reflecting Shang precedents)

## Key Academic Works

- David N. Keightley, *The Ancestral Landscape: Time, Space, and Community in Late Shang China* (2000)
- David N. Keightley, *Sources of Shang History* (1978)
- K.C. Chang, *Shang Civilization* (1980)
- Edward Shaughnessy — early Chinese paleography and history
- Robert Bagley — Shang bronze art
