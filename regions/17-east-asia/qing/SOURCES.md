# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Qing Veritable Records (Shilu) — massive official archive
- *Dream of the Red Chamber* (Cao Xueqin) — trans. David Hawkes (5 vols., Penguin)
- Commissioner Lin Zexu's memorial on the opium trade
- Zeng Guofan's diary and letters

## Key Academic Works

- Jonathan Spence, *The Search for Modern China* (1990) — comprehensive
- Evelyn Rawski, *The Last Emperors* (1998) — Qing as Manchu state
- Pamela Kyle Crossley, *The Manchus* (1997)
- Susan Naquin and Evelyn Rawski, *Chinese Society in the Eighteenth Century* (1987)
- Philip Kuhn, *Soulstealers: The Chinese Sorcery Scare of 1768* (1990)

## Needed

- Recent scholarship on the Qing in Inner Asia (Xinjiang, Mongolia, Tibet)
- Taiping Rebellion local history and demographic studies
