# CLEAN — E-2 Voice Keeper
## The Network's Height | Kuikuro Era 2

---

**Baseline voice** (established in opening): Patient, maintenance-attentive, matter-of-fact about ceremony and labor, internally observant, no editorializing. Short and medium sentences. No em-dashes. The voice of a community that thinks through its hands.

**Em-dash count (full chapter):** 0. Well within limit. A genuine property of the voice.

**Em-dash count (opening):** 0.

**Voice consistency scan:**

**Section by section:**

- **Opening (road-clearing):** Voice holds completely. "The road does not care about ceremony. The road cares only about being a road." — this is the voice at its cleanest.

- **The Garden Beneath the Garden:** Voice shifts slightly toward the analytical in one place. The sentence "This is not guesswork. It is a knowledge system with centuries of accumulated observation behind it, held in the hands of women who have never written anything down and who have maintained the most productive soil in the upper Xingu basin for as long as anyone's grandmother's grandmother can remember." The phrase "knowledge system" is a slight analytical intrusion — it is the author's vocabulary, not Tüke's world. However, the sentence immediately grounds the abstraction in the specific ("held in the hands of women who have never written anything down") and the generational memory is given in Tüke's own temporal terms ("as long as anyone's grandmother's grandmother can remember"). **Finding (P3):** "knowledge system" — author may decide. The sentence works; the phrase is borderline.

- **Arrival:** Voice holds. "The sound of the upper Xingu network in assembly is the sound of at least four language groups talking at once" — this is a slight slide toward the narrator's overview but is brief and returns quickly to the inside view (the trading language "running underneath as a current").

- **The Hüge Prepares:** Voice holds strongly. The sentence "This is what the hüge knows: not written, not diagrammed, not reducible to instruction" — brief analytical mode, but the parallel clauses carry the Garden-Town voice's pattern (short declarative beats) and return immediately to the embodied ("It lives in his ear and his voice").

- **The Ceremony's Work:** Voice holds. The wrestling analysis ("The wrestling is not purely ceremonial. It is a medium for communicating information about the network's relative capacities that is difficult to communicate any other way") slides toward the analytical. However, this is the chapter's structural-engine moment — it must show the wrestling doing its network-maintenance work, and the inside voice at this point is the voice of the anetü who is watching and registering. The word "capacities" is slightly modern but may be accepted.

- **What the Road Carries Home:** Voice holds until the final three paragraphs. The passage beginning "Something is coming that the network was not built to survive" is a departure from the chapter's consistent inside perspective. This is the chapter's only moment of narrator intrusion — the view from above, knowing what the people do not know. **Finding (P2):** The passage "Three thousand kilometers to the east... No one at Kuhikugu knows this. The anetü does not know it..." — this is modern-narrator omniscience. It works emotionally and structurally. It is the chapter's deliberate choice to name the future at the era's edge. But it is a voice shift and must be acknowledged as such. The GATE-2 scorer noted it and called it "correct." Judgment call for author: the shift is purposeful and brief, and the chapter returns to the inside voice immediately after.

- **Landing:** Voice returns to baseline perfectly. "The road does not care about ceremony. The road cares only about being a road." — the voice restores itself exactly.

**Summary:**
- 0 em-dashes (opening), 0 em-dashes (full chapter)
- 1 P2 finding: the narrator-omniscience passage in "What the Road Carries Home"
- 2 P3 findings: "knowledge system" in garden section; "capacities" in ceremony section
- All three are purposeful departures, not accidents. Author to confirm.

**CLEAN-E2 RESULT: Voice is sustained. Three findings for author review; all are purposeful departures that the author should explicitly confirm rather than fix.**
