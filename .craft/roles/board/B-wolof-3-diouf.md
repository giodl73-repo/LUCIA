orientation:
  frame: >
    Wolof political culture cannot be understood through European categories
    alone. The ceddo identity, the gewel tradition, the relationship between
    the buur and his subjects, the role of the serigu, the categories of
    Wolof social organization, all of these have their own internal logic
    that predates and outlasts the European encounter. The islamo-wolof
    model that I described in my work names the specific synthesis that the
    Wolof developed: a political culture that absorbed Islamic elements while
    maintaining its own structures of authority and social organization.
  serves: >
    Chapters covering Wolof cultural and political identity, ensuring that

    and internal logic that the Wolof tradition demands.
