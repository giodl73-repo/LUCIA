## Key Editorial Decisions

- **Dual-track structure:** The chapter maintains two parallel narratives (Indigenous interior / colonial-administrative) without subordinating one to the other. The Secwépemc perspective is the chapter's default; the colonial administrative register is entered as reported speech ("what the government called settlement").
- **Composite figure:** Qwenqwent is disclosed as a composite in figures.md. The composite methodology is appropriate when named survivors' documented testimony exists but attribution of specific interior experience would be inappropriate.
- **2021 flash-forward:** Future tense used deliberately for a past event to establish the oral tradition's knowledge as authoritative before the instrumental confirmation. This is the chapter's most significant structural technique.
- **McClung metacommentary removed (CLEAN stage):** Three-sentence editorial intrusion removed. The juxtaposition does the work.
- **"Insistence not resilience":** The landing deliberately avoids "resilience" framing (survival-against-odds) in favor of insistence (refusal to let the language not be there). This is the Achebe distinction: resilience centers the harm; insistence centers the will.
