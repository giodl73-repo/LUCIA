# B-Taíno-3 — Pané Scholar (Arrom/Keegan tradition)

## Background

The scholarship interpreting Ramón Pané's *Relación* — particularly José Juan Arrom's critical edition and William Keegan's synthesis — represents the primary scholarly apparatus for interpreting what Pané recorded. This board position represents that interpretive tradition: what the mythology actually says, how it has been understood by scholars who have worked closely with the Pané text, and where the chapter's rendering is consistent with or departs from the recorded tradition.

## Key Question

**"Is the mythology rendered as recorded, and is Pané's own position in the story made clear?"**
