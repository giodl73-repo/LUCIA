# Sources — Mangbetu

## Primary

- Schweinfurth, Georg. *The Heart of Africa: Three Years' Travels and Adventures in the Unexplored Regions of Central Africa*. 2 vols. 1873. — the essential first-hand account of the Mangbetu court
- Casati, Gaetano. *Ten Years in Equatoria and the Return with Emin Pasha*. 1891. — later explorer account
- Belgian colonial administrative records — Archives africaines, Brussels

## Academic

- Schildkrout, Enid & Keim, Curtis A. *African Reflections: Art from Northeastern Zaire*. 1990. American Museum of Natural History. — comprehensive study of Mangbetu art and culture
- Keim, Curtis A. "Women in Slavery Among the Mangbetu c. 1800–1910." In *Women and Slavery in Africa*, ed. Robertson & Klein. 1983. University of Wisconsin Press.
- Vansina, Jan. *Introduction à l'ethnographie du Congo*. 1966. Éditions Universitaires du Congo.
- Biebuyck, Daniel P. *The Arts of Central Africa: An Annotated Bibliography*. 1987. G.K. Hall. — bibliography for the broader region

## Needed (ACQUISITION)

- Mangbetu oral historical traditions — poorly documented; any recent collection work
- Studies on the head-binding practice and its social meaning specifically
- Analysis of the Mangbetu musical tradition and its instruments
- Archaeological surveys of the Uele valley capital sites
