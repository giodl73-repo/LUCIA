# Sources — Kievan Rus

## Primary Sources

- *Povest' Vremennykh Let* (Primary Chronicle / Tale of Bygone Years) — compiled c. 1113; the fundamental Rus historical text; attributed to Nestor the Chronicler
- *Russkaya Pravda* (Russian Truth) — the law code of Yaroslav the Wise; short and expanded versions
- Constantine VII Porphyrogennetos, *De Administrando Imperio* — c. 950; Byzantine emperor's description of the Varangian-Greek route
- *Slovo o polku Ihorevi* (The Lay of Igor's Campaign) — c. 1187; the great Rus literary text; the steppe frontier in verse
- Byzantine chronicles on the Rus — Leo the Deacon, the Skylitzes Chronicle
- *Life of Princess Olga* — hagiographic account of Kyiv's first Christian ruler
