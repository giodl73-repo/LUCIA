## Scorecard

| Scene     | 5/5   | Opens on James I at the walls of Valencia, spring 1238 — specific person, specific moment, geographically and historically anchored within the first sentence. The acequia channels, orange groves, flat-roofed towers: the landscape is present from the first paragraph. |

## Notes

This opening passes GATE-1 at 29/30 — well above the 24/30 threshold.

Particular strengths:
- The structural reversal — a king who has won needing his opponents to stay — establishes pactisme as lived political logic before it is ever named.
- The *Llibre dels Fets* as primary source is used without being foregrounded as a source; it functions as character witness.
- The closing "shape of it" earns its weight because the whole opening has been about shaping: compact, negotiation, arrangement.

One minor flag for PANEL-1:
- Voice dimension: the extended quotation of the compact formula may be slightly documentary in a passage that should remain interior to James. Panel-1 may wish to assess whether the formula integrates fully or sits as a slightly external citation.
