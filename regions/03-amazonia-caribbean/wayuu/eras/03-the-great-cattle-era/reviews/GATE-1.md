## Notes

The em-dash count: 0. The voice discipline is operating from the first sentence.

The hook established by the team lead's brief — "the meanwhile is most of life" — is turned into the chapter's organizing logic rather than used as a decorative closing line. The mochila and the wait for rain are both instances of the meanwhile: productive work in the absence of the thing you are waiting for. This structural logic will carry into the chapter.

One potential challenge for PANEL-1: the opening is so grounded in the intimate, domestic scale that the larger structural question (how cattle transformed the Guajira's political economy and reinforced Wayúu independence) is only gestured at in the penultimate section. The panel may want more early indication of the era's scale and stakes. Monitor whether Ibn Khaldun pushes on this.
