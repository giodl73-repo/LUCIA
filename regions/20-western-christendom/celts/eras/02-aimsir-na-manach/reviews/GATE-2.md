Dual-technology structural engine:** The chapter's engine is not a single cultural mechanism but the relationship between two parallel systems (oral-filí and written-monastic) whose mutual dependency is itself the structural argument. This is a new instance of engine-as-system-relationship rather than engine-as-single-mechanism. The terminal condition (embodied-transmission vulnerability) applies specifically to one system within the dual engine, which is what gives the ending its particular weight. Logging to INNOVATIONS.md for review.

The legal system is not background; it is the plot's mechanism. This extends the v1.5 Knowledge System at Method Level amendment to a sustained chapter-length application. Logging for review.
