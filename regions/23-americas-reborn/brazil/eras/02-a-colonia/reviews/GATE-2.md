## Notes

**Perfect score rationale:** The chapter achieves everything the v1.6 Chronicle rubric asks for simultaneously and without apparent effort. The immersion never breaks. The evidence is dense and verifiable. The progression is structural, not chronological. The figures are dimensional — the Ganga Zumba section is the chapter's sharpest moment, holding two correct positions in genuine tension without resolving them. The voice is the culture's own form performed at chapter length. The landing is the only landing this chapter could have.

**Potential innovation for the log:**

1. **Call-and-Response as Structural Engine and Analytical Instrument (Voice/Progression):** The chapter deploys the call-and-response form not decoratively but structurally — it appears three times with three different forms (explicit Q&A, structural echo in prose rhythm, triple-response expansion at landing). Each appearance is analytically different: the first demonstrates the knowledge system, the second enacts it in the transmission context, the third performs it as thesis delivery. This is a new application of the validated Call-and-Response register (African Diaspora) at chapter length. Candidate for Innovation Log.

2. **Dual Terminal Condition — Political Fall / Knowledge Vindication (Progression):** The chapter holds two simultaneous terminal conditions from the v1.6 framework: Substrate Exhaustion (the political community of Palmares eroded through demographic and military defeat) and Vindicating (the axé's transmission system was built to survive exactly this kind of rupture; the fall proves the design worked). The two terminal conditions apply to two different engines running simultaneously. This may be a new structural technique — the Dual Engine with Divergent Terminal Conditions. Candidate for Innovation Log.
