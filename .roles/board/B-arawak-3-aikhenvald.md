# B-3 — Alexandra Aikhenvald (Arawak Linguistics)

## Background

Alexandra Aikhenvald's work on Arawak includes the typological study "Arawak" in *The Amazonian Languages* (Cambridge UP, 1999) and extensive fieldwork on Arawak-family languages. Her linguistic analysis provides evidence for the migration sequence that complements the ceramic evidence: dialect divergence between island and continental Arawak-family languages is consistent with the timeline the ceramics suggest, with sufficient divergence to mark generations of separation but sufficient retention to confirm common origin.

## What She Will Praise
