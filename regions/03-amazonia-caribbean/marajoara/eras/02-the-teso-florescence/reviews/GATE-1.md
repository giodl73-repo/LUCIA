## Notes

The opening meets and slightly exceeds the 24/30 target. The single point deduction in Voice reflects one moment where the prose steps slightly outside to calibrate for the reader ("what outsiders will later call...") — a navigational gesture that could be removed without loss.

The core innovation: the urn prepared before death is not framed as strange or exotic. It is simply the logic of a culture that has already organized the question of continuity differently. This is Honesty at v1.2 standard — the no-verdict discipline feels like it belongs to the Marajoara, not to editorial constraint.
