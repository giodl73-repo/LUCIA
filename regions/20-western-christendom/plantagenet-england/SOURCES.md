# Sources — Plantagenet England

## Primary Sources

- *Magna Carta* (1215, reissued 1216, 1217, 1225) — the foundational constitutional document
- Glanvill, *Tractatus de Legibus et Consuetudinibus Regni Angliae* — c. 1187; the first systematic English law treatise
- Bracton, *De Legibus et Consuetudinibus Angliae* — c. 1235; the great common law treatise; "the king is under God and the law"
- Roger of Hoveden, *Chronica* — detailed record of Henry II and Richard I's reigns
- Roger of Wendover and Matthew Paris, *Chronica Majora* — 13th century; the fullest chronicle record
- *Domesday Book* continuations and Exchequer records — the administrative record
- Froissart, *Chronicles* — 14th-century account of the early Hundred Years War
- William Langland, *Piers Plowman* — c. 1370; the social world of Plantagenet England from below
- Geoffrey Chaucer, *Canterbury Tales* — c. 1390s; the literary masterpiece of the age
