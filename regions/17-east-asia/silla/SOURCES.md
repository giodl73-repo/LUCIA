# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Samguk Sagi* — Silla annals
- *Samguk Yusa* — Buddhist tales and Silla traditions
- Wonhyo's writings — *Awakening of Faith Commentary* and others; partially trans.
- Gyeongju tomb excavation reports (National Museum of Korea)

## Key Academic Works

- Ki-baik Lee, *A New History of Korea* (1984)
- Robert Buswell — Korean Buddhism, especially Wonhyo
- Jonathan Best — Silla art and culture
- Hyung Il Pai — Silla material culture
- Mark Peterson — Korean social history

## Needed

- Seokguram grotto archaeological and art historical studies
- Studies on the Silla-Tang War (670–676 CE)
- Recent bone-rank system analysis and social history
