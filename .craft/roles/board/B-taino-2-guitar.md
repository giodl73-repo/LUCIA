# B-Taíno-2 — Lynne Guitar

## Background

Lynne Guitar is one of the principal scholars of Taíno cultural continuity. Her collaborative work with Pedro Ferbel-Azcarate and Jorge Estevez documented the persistence of Taíno-descended communities and cultural practices that survived the colonial disruption. Her work established the scholarly basis for challenging the "extinction" narrative before the 2018 genome study provided genetic confirmation.
