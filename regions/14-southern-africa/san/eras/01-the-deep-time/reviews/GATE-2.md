**Notes:** The chapter passes GATE-2 decisively (52/60, well above the 48 threshold). Combined score of 81/90 (90%) exceeds the 80% target.

**Innovation flagged:** The "composite figure with honest disclosure" technique. The chapter uses composite figures (/Twi!kxao, !Nai) drawn from multiple ethnographic accounts, and it acknowledges this explicitly rather than hiding it. This turns an epistemological limitation (we cannot name a specific healer at a specific trance dance on a specific night) into a narrative technique that respects both the reader's intelligence and the culture's reality. The disclosure does not break immersion because it is woven into the figures.md rather than interrupting the chapter's flow. This technique may be relevant to any chapter where the primary record does not name individuals.
