## Innovations Flagged

**1. Outsider-as-Evidence for Structural Position (Progression dimension):** The Portuguese sources are used not as authorities on the Wolof but as evidence of the Wolof's structural position. Zurara's "bafflement" characterizes Portuguese limitations. Barros's account of Bemoim's death becomes evidence of Portuguese unreliability as allies. The outside source's biases become data about the STRUCTURAL RELATIONSHIP between the two parties, extending the v1.2 Honesty technique (Outsider's Bias as Inside Characterization) into the Progression dimension.

**2. Ecological Mechanism as Structural Engine (Progression dimension):** The tsetse fly and horse mortality are not background ecology but the material foundation of the entire political argument. The horse's 3-5 year lifespan makes cavalry a flow, not a stock, which makes the supply route (Saharan vs. Atlantic) the decisive political variable. Ecology becomes plot. This extends the v1.1 Structural Engine principle by grounding it in a specific ecological constraint.
