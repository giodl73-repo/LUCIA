# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Lao royal chronicles (*Phongsawadan Lao*) — compiled later but preserving early traditions
- That Luang stupa inscriptions
- Vietnamese and Chinese diplomatic records — Lan Xang's regional relations
- European missionary accounts (17th century) — particularly Jesuit sources

## Key Academic Works

- Martin Stuart-Fox, *A History of Laos* (1997) — standard scholarly history
- Martin Stuart-Fox, *The Lao Kingdom of Lan Xang: Rise and Decline* (1998)
- Søren Ivarsson, *Creating Laos: The Making of a Lao Space Between Indochina and Siam* (2008)
- Grant Evans — Lao identity and history
- Patrice Ladwig — Lao Theravada Buddhism

## Needed

- Studies on Vientiane and Luang Prabang as distinct court cultures
- Analysis of the Lan Xang split into three kingdoms (1707) and its consequences
- Research on the material culture of the Lan Xang court
