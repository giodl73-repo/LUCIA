# Sources — Carolingians / Franks

## Primary Sources

- *Vita Karoli Magni* (Life of Charlemagne) by Einhard — c. 830 CE; contemporary biography by a palace school alumnus
- *Capitulare de Villis* — c. 800; royal directive on estate management; window into Carolingian administrative ambition
- *Capitulary of Herstal* (779) and *Admonitio Generalis* (789) — core reform directives
- *Annales Regni Francorum* — the Royal Frankish Annals; official court record 741–829
- *Strasbourg Oaths* (842) — first documents in Old French and Old High German
- *De Ordine Palatii* by Hincmar of Reims — description of Carolingian court organization
- Letters of Alcuin — extensive correspondence illuminating the palace school and its program
- *Poeta Saxo* and other court poetry — the Carolingian literary renaissance in verse
