# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Marco Polo, *The Travels* — trans. Ronald Latham (Penguin) or Henry Yule
- Ibn Battuta, *Rihla* — the China sections; trans. H.A.R. Gibb
- Rashid al-Din, *Jami' al-Tawarikh* — Persian history of the Mongols

## Key Academic Works

- John Dardess, *Conquerors and Confucians: Aspects of Political Change in Late Yuan China* (1973)
- Elizabeth Endicott-West — Yuan administrative history
- David Morgan, *The Mongols* (2nd ed., 2007)
- Morris Rossabi, *Khubilai Khan: His Life and Times* (1988)
- Paul Buell — Mongol food culture and *Yinshan Zhengyao*
