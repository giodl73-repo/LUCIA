## Innovations Flagged for INNOVATIONS.md

**Innovation 1 — Source Asymmetry: Dual-Column as Institution-Building**
The dual-column register (Arabic original / Catalan translation) is used not merely as a source-asymmetry acknowledgment but as the mechanism by which the compact machine operated in practice. The discrepancies between columns generated disputes; the disputes generated rulings; the rulings generated precedents; the precedents became the Tribunal de las Aguas. The source gap IS the institution-building process, made visible through a specific material artifact. This extends the v1.5 Source Asymmetry framework to the institutional-genesis level — the asymmetry between two knowledge systems (Islamic acequia law, Catalan notarial law) is the engine of the new institution, not merely the evidentiary condition of the chapter.
- Dimension: Evidence / Source Asymmetry (v1.5 extension)
- Source: Aragon Era 2, Section 2

**Innovation 2 — Institutional Persistence as Landing (extension of v1.1)**
The Tribunal de las Aguas is a living court — still operating in Valencia, still hearing disputes in the open air beside the cathedral, still maintaining acequia distribution schedules. Using a continuous legal institution (not an oral tradition, not a linguistic structure, but a functioning court) as the persistence mechanism in the landing extends the v1.1 "culture's own persistence as close" to the juridical-institutional category. The acequia amin's register in 1238 and the Tribunal's meeting in 2026 are connected by an unbroken procedural chain. This may be the first chapter in LUCIA to use a continuously operating legal institution as the landing's persistence mechanism.
- Dimension: Landing / Persistence Mechanism (v1.1 extension)
- Source: Aragon Era 2, Landing section
