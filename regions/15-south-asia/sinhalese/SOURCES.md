# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Mahavamsa* — "The Great Chronicle"; trans. Wilhelm Geiger (Pali Text Society, 1912)
- *Culavamsa* — continuation of the chronicle; trans. Wilhelm Geiger
- *Dipavamsa* — earlier chronicle; trans. Hermann Oldenberg
- Robert Knox, *An Historical Relation of the Island Ceylon* (1681) — captive's account of the Kandyan kingdom

## Key Academic Works

- K.M. de Silva, *A History of Sri Lanka* (1981)
- R.A.L.H. Gunawardana, *Robe and Plough: Monasticism and Economic Interest in Early Medieval Sri Lanka* (1979)
- John Clifford Holt — Buddhist kingship in Sri Lanka
- Ananda Guruge — Ashokan Buddhism and Sri Lanka connection
- Wilhelm Geiger — Pali studies and Mahavamsa scholarship
