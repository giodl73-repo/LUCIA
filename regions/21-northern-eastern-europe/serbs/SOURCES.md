# Sources — Serbs

## Primary Sources

- *Žitije Stefana Nemanje* (Life of Stefan Nemanja) by Sava — the hagiographic account of the dynasty's founder
- *Zakonik Cara Stefana Dušana* (Law Code of Tsar Stefan Dušan, 1349/1354) — the empire's legal text
- Kosovo cycle oral epic poetry — collected in the 18th–19th centuries by Vuk Stefanović Karadžić; preserves earlier oral tradition
- *Letopis Popa Dukljanina* — 12th century; the chronicle of the Zeta princes
- Byzantine chronicles on Serbian affairs — Niketas Choniates, John Kantakouzenos
- The Hilandar monastery archives — the richest collection of medieval Serbian documents; on Mount Athos
