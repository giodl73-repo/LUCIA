# Sources — Colombia

## Key Academic Works
- Gabriel García Márquez, *Cien Años de Soledad* (1967) — primary source as much as literature; the Caribbean coast as felt history
- Marco Palacios, *Between Legitimacy and Violence: A History of Colombia, 1875–2002* (2006, Duke) — the standard political history
- María Clemencia Ramírez — FARC, state, and the periphery; *Between the Guerrillas and the State* (2011)
- Forrest Hylton, *Evil Hour in Colombia* (2006) — the political economy of conflict
- Gonzalo Sánchez Gómez & Donny Meertens — La Violencia; *Bandits, Peasants, and Politics* (2001)
- María Victoria Uribe — massacres and political violence

## Primary Sources
- García Márquez, *Living to Tell the Tale* (memoir, 2002) — the Caribbean coast and the political world that formed him
- The Colombian Constitution of 1991
- The 2016 FARC-Government Peace Agreement
- Reports of the Centro Nacional de Memoria Histórica (National Center for Historical Memory)
