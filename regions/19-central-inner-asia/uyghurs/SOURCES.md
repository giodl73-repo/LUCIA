# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Karabalghasun inscription (821 CE) — trilingual (Chinese, Sogdian, Old Türkic) account of the Uyghur khaganate
- Turfan manuscript collections (Manichaean, Buddhist, administrative texts in Uyghur)
- Dunhuang cave library manuscripts — Uyghur texts among the Stein and Pelliot finds
- Chinese Tang dynasty records — the silk-cavalry exchange, the An Lushan Rebellion campaigns

## Key Academic Works

- Colin Mackerras, *The Uighur Empire According to the T'ang Dynastic Histories* (1972)
- Peter Golden, *An Introduction to the History of the Turkic Peoples* (1992)
- Étienne de la Vaissière — Sogdian-Uyghur commercial networks
- Hamilton, *Manuscrits ouïgours du IXe–Xe siècle de Touen-houang* (1986)
- Thomas Allsen — Uyghur administrators in the Mongol empire
