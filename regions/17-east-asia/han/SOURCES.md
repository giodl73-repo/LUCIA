# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Sima Qian, *Shiji* (Records of the Grand Historian) — trans. Burton Watson (3 vols., Columbia)
- Ban Gu, *Hanshu* (Book of Han) — partially trans. Homer Dubs and others
- Faxian, *Faxian zhuan* (A Record of Buddhistic Kingdoms) — trans. James Legge

## Key Academic Works

- Michael Loewe, *A Biographical Dictionary of the Qin, Former Han and Xin Periods* (2000)
- Michael Loewe, *Everyday Life in Early Imperial China* (1968)
- Hans Bielenstein — Han administrative history
- Hans van Ess — Han intellectual history and the Confucian state
- Endymion Wilkinson, *Chinese History: A Manual* (2000) — reference

## Needed

- Recent Mawangdui and other Han-era excavation studies
- Silk Road studies connecting Han to the Parthian and Roman worlds
