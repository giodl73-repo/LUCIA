Strong chapter across all dimensions. Voice is the standout achievement — the Court-Translational register is sustained at the highest level across the chapter's full length. The structural engine (library as knowledge-accumulation machine) drives the arc without being announced; the reader arrives at the Vindicating terminal condition through narrative rather than through argument.

**Innovation flag — Court-Translational Voice Register:** The register deployed here — bilingual in its intellectual reference world, precise and observational, moving between Arabic scholarly vocabulary and Persian poetic tradition without breaking from either — has not previously been named in the Voice Spectrum. It is distinct from "Urban/scholarly" (Abbasid), which tends toward density and layering in a single language; from "Philosophical-Devotional" (Tibetan), which builds meaning clause by clause; and from "Argumentative/Text-Centered" (Talmudic), which questions and accumulates. The Court-Translational register's defining feature is its bilingual fluency as a structural property: the prose itself inhabits two intellectual traditions simultaneously, and the voice's authority comes from that double inhabitation. Candidate for addition to the Voice Spectrum in STYLE-GUIDE.md. Log to INNOVATIONS.md.

The word carries cosmological meaning, operates as the era's central question, and delivers the landing without the author stepping in to explain it.
