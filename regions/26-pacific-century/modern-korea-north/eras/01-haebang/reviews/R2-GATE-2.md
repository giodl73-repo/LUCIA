## Innovation Log Entry

**Innovation identified:** **Mythology as Structural Necessity** — The chapter operationalizes the v1.2 Honesty amendment at structural depth. The opening and chapter together achieve a register where the culture's own relationship to truth-telling becomes the organizing principle of the narrative. The state's intolerance for doubt is not presented as editorializing about the state; it is presented as the state's own epistemic requirement. This goes beyond the v1.2 technique of "Open Silence" or "Outsider's Bias as Inside Characterization." This is a full chapter built on the principle that a state built on mythology must enforce mythology as truth, and this enforcement is not aberration — it is the state's central mechanism.

This technique extends beyond North Korea. Any culture whose legitimacy rests on a founding story that cannot be questioned would operate similarly. The machinery of enforced belief is not unique to communism; it is a feature of states that lack other sources of legitimacy (historical precedent, democratic process, inherited law). The innovation worth logging: **Founding Mythology as State Operating System** — where the chapter shows that the mythology is not a lie that could be abandoned if truth became available; it is the permanent operating system because the state's survival depends on it.

Recommend considering if this innovation should trigger discussion of v1.5 amendment to Progression or Evidence dimensions.
