# Sources — Meroe

## Primary / Archaeological

- Meroe pyramid cemetery excavations — Reisner, Harvard-Boston Expedition; ongoing work by various missions
- Naga temple complex — ongoing excavations by Museum of Egyptian and Islamic Art, Berlin
- Musawwarat es-Sufra excavations — Humboldt University, Berlin; the Great Enclosure
- Meroitic inscriptions — corpus assembled by Griffith; ongoing database work
- Res Gestae Divi Augusti — Roman primary source; Augustus's account of the Meroitic conflict

## Academic

- Welsby, Derek A. *The Kingdom of Kush: The Napatan and Meroitic Empires*. 1996. British Museum Press.
- Török, László. *Meröe City, an Ancient African Capital*. 1997. Egypt Exploration Society.
- Wildung, Dietrich (ed.). *Sudan: Ancient Kingdoms of the Nile*. 1997. Flammarion.
- Haynes, Joyce L. *Nubia: Ancient Kingdoms of Africa*. 1992. Museum of Fine Arts, Boston.
- Rilly, Claude. *Le méroïtique et sa famille linguistique*. 2010. Peeters. — Meroitic language and script decipherment progress
- Trigger, Bruce. *Nubia Under the Pharaohs*. 1976. Thames & Hudson.
