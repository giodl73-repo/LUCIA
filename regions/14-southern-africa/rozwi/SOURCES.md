# Sources — Rozwi (Changamire)

## Primary / Archaeological

- Khami excavation records — Robinson, K.R.; the major archaeological study
- Naletale survey records — National Museums and Monuments of Zimbabwe
- Portuguese records — the documentation of the Changamire expulsion from the Portuguese perspective

## Academic

- Beach, D.N. *The Shona and Zimbabwe 900–1850*. 1980. Heinemann.
- Mudenge, S.I.G. *A Political History of Munhumutapa*. 1988. Zimbabwe Publishing House. — Changamire in context
- Randles, W.G.L. *The Empire of Monomotapa from the Fifteenth to the Nineteenth Century*. 1981. Mambo Press.
- Robinson, K.R. *Khami Ruins: Report on Excavations Undertaken for the Commission for the Preservation of Natural and Historical Monuments and Relics, Southern Rhodesia*. 1959. Cambridge University Press.
- Pikirayi, Innocent. *The Zimbabwe Culture*. 2001. AltaMira Press.
