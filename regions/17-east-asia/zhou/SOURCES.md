# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Lunyu* (Analects of Confucius) — trans. D.C. Lau (Penguin) or Edward Slingerland
- *Mencius* — trans. D.C. Lau (Penguin)
- *Daodejing* (Laozi) — trans. D.C. Lau or Roger Ames
- *Zhuangzi* — trans. Burton Watson
- *Sunzi Bingfa* (Art of War) — trans. Victor Mair (Columbia)
- *Shijing* (Book of Songs) — trans. Arthur Waley or Kang-i Sun Chang

## Key Academic Works

- Mark Edward Lewis, *Writing and Authority in Early China* (1999)
- Mark Edward Lewis, *Sanctioned Violence in Early China* (1990)
- Lothar von Falkenhausen — Western Zhou archaeology
- Edward Shaughnessy — early Chinese texts and history
- Constance Cook and John Major (eds.), *Defining Chu* (1999)
