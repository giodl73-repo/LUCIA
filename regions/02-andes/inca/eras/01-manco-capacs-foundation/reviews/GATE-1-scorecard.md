**Notes:** Passes Gate-1 (24+ required). The opening establishes the vertical ecology, the quipu, and the origin myth as the three pillars of the chapter. The quipu passage is the opening's strongest moment — it establishes the non-writing record system as a central theme of the book without defensiveness or apology.
