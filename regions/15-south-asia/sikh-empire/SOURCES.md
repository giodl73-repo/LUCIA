# Sources

## Primary Sources

- *Guru Granth Sahib* — the complete scripture; trans. Manmohan Singh (8 vols.)
- *Bhai Gurdas Vaaran* — early Sikh theological poetry
- Victor Jacquemont, *Letters from India* (1829–1832) — French traveler at Ranjit Singh's court
- Ranjit Singh's administrative records — partially published by Sikh History Society

## Key Academic Works

- J.S. Grewal, *The Sikhs of the Punjab* (1990, New Cambridge History of India)
- Khushwant Singh, *A History of the Sikhs* (2 vols., 1963, 1966)
- Purnima Dhavan, *When Sparrows Became Hawks* (2011) — Khalsa militarization
- Louis Fenech and W.H. McLeod, *Historical Dictionary of Sikhism* (2014)
- Christophe Jaffrelot — Punjab and Sikh identity in modern context
