# Sources — Chinese Overseas (Huaqiao)

## Key Academic Works
- Wang Gungwu, *China and the Chinese Overseas* (1991, Times Academic Press) — the definitive overview by the leading scholar
- Yen Ching-Hwang, *A Social History of the Chinese in Singapore and Malaya, 1800–1911* (1986)
- Adam McKeown, *Chinese Migrant Networks and Cultural Change* (2001, Chicago) — the transnational networks
- Lynn Pan, ed., *The Encyclopedia of the Chinese Overseas* (1998, Harvard) — the comprehensive reference
- Evelyn Hu-DeHart — Chinese in Latin America; the coolie trade
- K.C. Chang — Chinese food culture as diaspora marker

## Primary Sources
- Zheng He's voyage records — incomplete; primary sources in *Mingshi* and *Yingyai Shenglan*
- Huiguan records — clan and dialect association archives; partially preserved in Singapore and Malaysia
- Chinese-language newspapers from diaspora communities (19th–20th century)
- Congressional records on the Chinese Exclusion Act (1882)
