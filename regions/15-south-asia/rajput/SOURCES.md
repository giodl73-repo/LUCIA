# Sources

## Primary Sources

- Rajput genealogical texts (Bhat and Charan oral traditions)
- *Prithviraj Raso* (Chand Bardai) — medieval Rajput bardic epic
- James Tod, *Annals and Antiquities of Rajasthan* (1829–1832) — colonial-era compilation with primary Rajput sources
- Mughal administrative sources on Rajput mansabdars (Ain-i-Akbari)

## Key Academic Works

- Dirk H.A. Kolff, *Naukar, Rajput and Sepoy* (1990)
- Ramya Sreenivasan, *The Many Lives of a Rajput Queen* (2007)
- Norman Ziegler — Rajput martial values and clan identity studies
- Cynthia Talbot, *The Last Hindu Emperor: Prithviraj Chauhan* (2016)
- Joanne Smith Finegan — Rajput art and patronage

## Needed

- Studies on Rajput fort architecture and urban planning
- Analysis of the jauhar tradition — sources and historiography
- Recent scholarship on Rajput-Mughal marriage alliances and their political consequences
